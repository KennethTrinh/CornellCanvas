Hi,

Our SciFi Lab is currently recruiting participants this weekend and next Monday for our research project: using commercial headsets to recognize silent speech.

Please note that this is not relevant to the course, but your help is greatly appreciated!

During the experiment, we will ask you to wear a pair of slightly modified commercial headsets (Bose QC 45) and silently mouth commands according to instructions on the screen while we play music through the headsets. The study should last 40-50 minutes and you will receive $15 compensation for completing the study. The study will take place at G29 Gates Hall .

To participate,

  • You must be 18 years or older.

  • Please expect audio/video recordings, including recordings of your face. These recordings will not be shared outside the research team of this project.

  • Please select a one-hour slot on this spreadsheet (you can just highlight it, if a slot has already been highlighted, it means it's not available): https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1G-LB1jEGdZK_BmN4W6tHP7puy7sGnL_pm18IJNxRToA/edit?usp=sharing

    • IRB protocol: IRB0009898 (2010009898)

    If you have any questions about the project, please contact the lead PhD student Ruidong Zhang ( rz379@cornell.edu) or Prof. Cheng Zhang ( chengzhang@cornell.edu ).

    Hope you have a great weekend!

    Best,
