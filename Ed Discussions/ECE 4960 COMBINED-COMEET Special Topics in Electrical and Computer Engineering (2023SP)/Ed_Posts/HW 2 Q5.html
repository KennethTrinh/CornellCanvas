You can verify that the graph in undirected by checking that A=A'. And yes for undirected graphs in and out degree are the same. As written in the question the plot should have the node labels in the x-axis and the centrality measures in the the y-axis (normalized so that they sum up to one). This is not a bar plot. It is a plot of a function f(x) where x is the node label and f is the corresponding value of centrality
