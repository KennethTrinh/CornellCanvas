In one-variable calculus integrals of continuous functions are defined as limits of Riemann sums. We proved in class that this agrees with the upper/lower sum definition of the Riemann integral. So we are now free to apply the theorems of one-variable calculus, such as the fundamental theorem, the substitution formula, etc.
