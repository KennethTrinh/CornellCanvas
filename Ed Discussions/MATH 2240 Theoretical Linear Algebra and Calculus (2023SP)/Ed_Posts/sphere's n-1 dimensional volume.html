Generalized spherical coordinates give you a weak parametrization

$$\gamma\colon[0,2\pi]\times\Bigl[-\frac12\pi,\frac12\pi\Bigr]^{n-2}\to\boldsymbol{S}^{n-1},$$

so the volume is an integral of a smooth function over a compact domain.
