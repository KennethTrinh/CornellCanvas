Additional comment: Sometimes LaTeX doesn't work out all formatting/references during a first compilation so it is a good idea to compile two or three times consecutively before submitting. Also any reasonable compiler produces extensive warnings and error messages, and it is a good idea to pay attention to those and see if you can address them. It is okay to have warnings as long as the output is good, but often warnings point to something going wrong that does affect the output.
