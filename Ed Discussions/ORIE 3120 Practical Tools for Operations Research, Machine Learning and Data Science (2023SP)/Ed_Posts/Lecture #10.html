The first “column” just indexes the rows and can be used to refer to a specific row number in the DataFrame. It’s honestly kind of arbitrary: you could reorder the records in the DataFrame and get different values for these.

The LocationID column gives us the identification for a specific zone which is more important to us, because we can join on this as you said.
