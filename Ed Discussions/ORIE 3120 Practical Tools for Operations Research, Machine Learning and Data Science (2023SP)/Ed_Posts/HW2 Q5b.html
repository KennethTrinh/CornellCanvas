To add to this, be careful that you don't bin the rounded unit prices, you should only bin the unit prices. For example, the occurrence of the roundedunitprice of 5 has a unit price of 4.5, which should be in bin center 0. But the occurrence of the roundedunitprice of 55 has a unit price of 55, which should be in bin center 60 (according to the original binning scheme).
