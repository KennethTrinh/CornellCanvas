Why would an inner join in this case result in the loss of records?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Ashley Zeng (staff)

Vote Count: 0

An inner join would only include the records where Majors.MajorID = Students.Major. A left join, on the other hand, includes all the records in the Majors table, even the majors with no students enlisted.
