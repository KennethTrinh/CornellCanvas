I would first check if your divisions are implemented correctly. This can sometimes cause weird errors when we do not ensure the numerators of our divisions are of type REAL. If you know python, think about this as an equivalent of float vs int division. Try using a CAST function in Q3a to ensure our numerators are of type REAL. Another fix for this is to multiply every numerator by 1.0
