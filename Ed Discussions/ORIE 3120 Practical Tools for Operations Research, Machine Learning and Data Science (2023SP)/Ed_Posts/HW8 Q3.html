You cannot change Xs but you can change betas. Recall, its a linear function of the parameters beta0, beta1, ... if its in the form of beta0*(function of inputs) + beta1*(function of inputs). For example, if I have Beta_0^2*X^2, I could change this to Beta_1*X^2 where Beta_1 = Beta_0^2
