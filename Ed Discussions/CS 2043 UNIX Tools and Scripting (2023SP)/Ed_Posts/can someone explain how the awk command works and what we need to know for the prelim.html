Here in the first photo. awk goes through each line of marks.txt and looks for what is in between the first pair of /. Which is Ron in this case, then once it finds a match (which would be the word Ron in line 1) it executes the statement after the second / which is print $4 which prints the 4th column/field in the line, in this case that would be 90. THE BOLDED LINE IS WHAT WE WERE TOLD TO KNOW FOR THE PRELIM
