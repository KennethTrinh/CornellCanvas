If you want to move a file from ugclinux to your VM, you should be doing your scp command within your VM terminal. Then you can do the command 
scp [netid]@132.236.91.186:/home/[netid]/[filename] .
This will copy your desired file from ugclinux to your "local" machine (in this case you should be doing it on your VM so it will appear as a file in your home directory on your VM. Then you can submit it to CMS
