I shared this with Lillian during our meeting today that she thought I should share with the whole class. I've been running my project models on the Cornell Center for Social Sciences (CCSS) remote servers. While training my neural network I received an email from the server moderator essentially telling me that I was a "high computing needs user" (aka a total CPU hog) and I therefore should be removed from the server so I didn't slow down everyone else in the process. My call out ended up being a great thing however, since they decided to set me up with my own personal cloud server to use! (Full disclosure that I work for CCSS so that probably has a big role in my happy ending rather than just getting completely booted off)
