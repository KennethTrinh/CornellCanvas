I highly doubt that the Slashdot and CMV datasets would cover this well enough (since they seem to be pretty tight communities and stick to English only), but I'm generally curious about what effects being in different cultures/contexts have on how different social effects play out, and I'd like to hear what others might have to share about these ideas.

Really broadly:

  • What impact does code-switching (thanks Lillian for introducing that word to me) have on the effectiveness of someone's communication? I think this includes things like a speaker use non-English words to appeal to a certain audience, or some forum poster using in-jokes (Internet memes?) to show how they belong.

  • On a super different track... how do some of the results that have been observed in English-language studies play out in other languages?
