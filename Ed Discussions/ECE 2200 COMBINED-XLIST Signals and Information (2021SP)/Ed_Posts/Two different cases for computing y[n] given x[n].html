We are told that if x[n] is a pure sinusoid, it does not have a frequency response and that causal FIRs always have a frequency response. Why are pure sinusoids not considered causal FIRs?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: David Delchamps (admin)

Vote Count: 1

I think you're conflating DTFTs and frequency responses. Systems, not signals, have frequency responses. Signals, not systems, have DTFTs. Pure sinusoidal signals don't have DTFTs. Causal FIR systems always have frequency responses.
