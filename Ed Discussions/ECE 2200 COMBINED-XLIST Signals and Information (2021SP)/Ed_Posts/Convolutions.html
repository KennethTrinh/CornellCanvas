I would suggest not doing this problem using convolutions but instead just figuring out by hand what the impulse response of the composite system is by using $\delta [ n ]$ as input signal and seeing what signal emerges from the first system, then plugging that into the I/O formula for the second system, etc., until you hit the output of the composite system.
