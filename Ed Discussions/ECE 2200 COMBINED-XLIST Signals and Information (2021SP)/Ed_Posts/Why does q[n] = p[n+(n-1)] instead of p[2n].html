The leftmost 1 in $q [ n ]$ happens at $n = - ( N - 1 )$. The correct specification for $q [ n ]$, about which some confusion has arisen, is $q [ n ] = \left \{ \begin{array}{cl} 1 & {\rm when} \; - N < n < N \\ 0 & {\rm else.} \end{array} \right .$ Thus we shift $q [ n ]$ to the right by $N - 1$ to plant that left-most 1 at $n = 0$.
