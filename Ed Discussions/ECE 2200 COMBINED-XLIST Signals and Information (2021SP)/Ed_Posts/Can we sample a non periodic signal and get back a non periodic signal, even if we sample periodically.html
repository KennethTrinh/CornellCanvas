Author: David Delchamps (admin)

Vote Count: 0

If you sample $x ( t )$ at $f _ s > 2 f _ {\rm max}$ and put the samples through an ideal D-to-C with interpolation interval $T _ s = 1 / f _ s$, then $x ( t )$ will emerge, whether $x ( t )$ is periodic or not.
