Not sure what you mean when you say "when graphing," but yeah, the standard form for a pure real-valued sinusoid is $A \cos ( 2 \pi f _ o t + \phi )$ where $A > 0$ and $\phi \in ( - \pi , \pi ]$, so adding or subtracting $\pi$ from the phase will always get you into that form when your "original" amplitude is negative.
