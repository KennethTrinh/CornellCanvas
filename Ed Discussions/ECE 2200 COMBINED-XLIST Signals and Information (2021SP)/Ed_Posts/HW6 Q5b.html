Yeah, this problem is all about transient vs. steady-state. The frequency response, which is a property of the system , doesn't change when you alter the input to the system. What changes is the output of the system. The piecewise thing you refer to tells you that when you use a sinusoid starting at 0, i.e. when you insert the unit step, the resulting output $y [ n ]$ is zero for $n < 0$, then goes through a transient epoch, then for $n > M$ is exactly what it would have been if you'd used a "forever sinusoid" as input. Your expression for that last piece will involve the frequency response.
