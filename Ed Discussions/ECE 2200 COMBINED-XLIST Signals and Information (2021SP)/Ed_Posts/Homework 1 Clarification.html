Hi everyone, just thought I'd mention a common mistake made on homework 1, question 5. When you write a cosine function as a sum of complex exponentials, make sure to include Re { } surrounding it. For example: cos (2*pi*f*t) = e^(j*2*pi*f*t) is incorrect cos (2*pi*f*t) = Re {e^(j*2*pi*f*t) } is correct! Good luck on your exam!
