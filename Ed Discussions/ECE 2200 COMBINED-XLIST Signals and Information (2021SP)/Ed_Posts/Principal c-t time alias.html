$f _ {\rm prin}$ is never negative. In Case $\alpha$, you "land at $f _ {\rm prin}$. In Case $\beta$, you "land" at $- f _ {\rm prin}$. In the part of the problem you're asking about, you "land$ at $- 200$ (i.e. Case $\beta$) , so $f _ {\rm prin} = 200$ and you have to negate the phase to get $x _ {\rm prin} ( t )$.
