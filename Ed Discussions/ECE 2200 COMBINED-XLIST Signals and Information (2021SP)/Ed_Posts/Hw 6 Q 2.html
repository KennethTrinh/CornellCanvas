Set x[n] = e^(jnw) and plug in to that equation for y[n]. Remember that when the input is of the form e^(jnw), the output will be H(w)*e^(jnw). Set them equal and solve for H(w). I don't think this has to be in sines and cosines; you can just leave it with the exponentials.
