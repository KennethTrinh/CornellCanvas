Author: David Delchamps (admin)

The impulse response of an FIR system with governing equation

$y [ n ] = \sum _ {k = 0} ^ M b _ k x [ n - k ]$

is always given by

$h [ n ] = \left \{ \begin{array}{cl} b _ n & {\rm when} \; 0 \leq n \leq M \\ 0 & {\rm otherwise.} \end{array} \right .$
