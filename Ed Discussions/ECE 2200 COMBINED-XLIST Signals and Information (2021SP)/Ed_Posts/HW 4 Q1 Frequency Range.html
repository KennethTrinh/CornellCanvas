This is one of the problems part of which depends on Tuesday's lecture, where we talk about finding all the continuous-time aliases of a sinusoid w.r.t. a given sampling frequency. For now, see whether this helps: $\cos ( 2 \pi ( f _ o - k 900 ) t + \phi )$ samples the same as $\cos ( 2 \pi f _ o t + \phi )$ even when $f _ o - k 900 < 0$. Also, remember that $\cos ( - \theta ) = \cos \theta$ for all real $\theta$.
