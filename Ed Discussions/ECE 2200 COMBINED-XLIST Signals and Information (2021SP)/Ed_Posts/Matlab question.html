According to the documentation for plus , vectors with different orientations (one row vector and one column vector) implicitly expand to form a matrix. This implicit expansion will also occur whenever MATLAB determines that two arrays have 'compatible' sizes. Check out the "Add Row and Column Vector" example in the second link below. https://www.mathworks.com/help/matlab/ref/plus.html https://www.mathworks.com/help/matlab/matlab_prog/compatible-array-sizes-for-basic-operations.html
