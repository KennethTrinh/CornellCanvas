I reasoned as follows: the first two sums' terms are all zero except possibly the $k = 4$-terms because of the $\delta [ k - 4 ]$. That term is $\delta [ n ]$ in the first sum and $\delta [ n - 2 ]$ in the second sum. Reason similarly about the third and fourth sums.
