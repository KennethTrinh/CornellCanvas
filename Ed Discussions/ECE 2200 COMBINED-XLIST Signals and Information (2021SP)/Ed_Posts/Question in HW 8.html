The way I do the $N$-point Cyclic Shift Rule requires $n _ o$ to satisfy $0 \leq n _ o < N$. The specifications "when $n = 8$" and "when $n \neq 8$" pre-suppose $0 \leq n < 10$. Note that the thing equals 1 not only when $n = 8$ but also when $\langle n \rangle _ {10} = 8$, but the only one of those $n$-values that "counts" in the world of 10-point signals is $n = 8$.
