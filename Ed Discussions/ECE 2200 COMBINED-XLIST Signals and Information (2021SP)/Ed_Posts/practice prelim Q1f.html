The output of the ideal D-to-C with interpolation interval $T _ s$ must have bandwidth less than $f _ 2 / 2$, which means we need $37 < f _ s / 2 = 1 / ( 2 T _ s )$. Accordingly, $74 T _ s < 1$, or $T _ s < 1 / 74$.
