The series is of the form $\sum _ {k = 0} ^ 9 \gamma ^ k$ with $\gamma = - e ^ {- j \widehat{\omega}}$.

Partial geometric says this is equal to

$ \frac{1 - \gamma ^ {10}}{1 - \gamma} = \frac{1 - e ^ {- j 10 \widehat{\omega}}}{1 + e ^ {- j \widehat{\omega}}}$.
