I suggest drawing a picture. Consider the two numbers $1 + j$ and $- 1 - j$. In both cases, $\tan ^ {- 1} ( y / x ) = \pi / 4$, but $1 + j = e ^ {j \pi / 4}$ while $- 1 - j = e ^ {- j 3 \pi / 4}$.
