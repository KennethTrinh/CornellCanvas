You don't convolve the two $y [ n ]$'s to get the difference equation for the cascade. You convolve the two systems' impulse responses and get the difference equation from that, or you just plug one of the $y [ n ]$'s into the other difference equation in place of $x [ n ]$ everywhere and clean it up.
