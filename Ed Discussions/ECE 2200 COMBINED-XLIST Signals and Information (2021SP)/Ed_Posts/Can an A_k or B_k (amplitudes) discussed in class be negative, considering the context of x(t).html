No, we always require the amplitudes to be positive. The phase will take care of signs. For example, $- 7 \cos ( 2 \pi ( 3 ) t ) = 7 \cos ( 2 \pi ( 3 ) t + \pi )$ --- the second way is how we choose to write it.
