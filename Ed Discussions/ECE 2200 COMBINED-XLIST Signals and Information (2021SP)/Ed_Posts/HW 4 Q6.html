Look at the series representation for the continuous-time signal. Look at individual values of $t$, say $0 \leq t < 1$ for example. You discover that only one or two of the terms in the infinite series determine the value of the continuous-time signal over that $t$-range. From those terms you can find an explicit expression for the continuous-time signal over that $t$-range,, and from that explicit expression the plot is easy.
