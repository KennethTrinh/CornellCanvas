Why do we want to use X(z)/z when we use partial fractions and just not X(z)?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: David Delchamps (admin)

Vote Count: 0

Because in the end you want $X ( z )$ itself as the sum of terms that look like $\frac{z}{z - z _ o}$ rather than $\frac{1}{z - z _ o}$ --- the latter form is what partial fractions gives you.
