Can you elaborate on how the key picture applies to this case where we're sampling below the Nyquist frequency?

------------------------------------

Author: David Delchamps (admin)

Vote Count: 0

The key picture says that $y ( t )$, the output of the D/C, is always the principal continuous-time alias of the input $x ( t )$ wrt the sampling frequency $f _ s$. If $f _ s$ happens to exceed the Nyquist rate of $x ( t )$, then $x ( t )$ is its own principal continuous-time alias wrt $f _s$, whereby $y ( t ) = x ( t )$.
