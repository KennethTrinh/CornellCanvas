You can't do "the power $f _ o k$" maneuver you refer to unless $f _ o k$ is an integer. It's a complex-number thing. Like for example, $j = e ^ {j \pi / 2} = e ^ {j 2 \pi ( 1 / 4 )} \neq 1 ^ {1 / 4} = 1$.
