Why h[n] is the sum of terms C0*Z0^n*u[n] when H(z) is C0Z/(Z-Z0)? Won't h[n] just be equal to C0*Z0^n*u[n] instead of sum of terms of C0*Z0^n*u[n]?

In this discussion, $H ( z )$ is the sum of terms $\frac{C _ o z}{z - z _ o}$, where $z _ o$ is a pole of $H ( z )$.
