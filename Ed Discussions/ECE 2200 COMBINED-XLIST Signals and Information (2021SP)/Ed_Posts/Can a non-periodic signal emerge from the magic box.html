Author: David Delchamps (admin)

That can happen, but not in the universe of pure sinusoids and linear combos thereof, which is where we live in ECE 2200.

Actually, a non-periodic signal can emerge even in the sinusoidal universe if the input to the box comes from sampling a sum of sinusoids with frequencies not harmonically related, in which case you deal with them one term at a time, and the output of the box might end up non-periodic.
