I have uploaded a couple of resource pages I'd like to draw your attention to, a document on homework guidelines , and my presentation on writing a PhD thesis, which has tons of info on creating effective LaTeX documentation, but also some good notes for Matlab and Inkscape.

The most important points that I want to bring to your attention;

  • Write the solution that you wish someone had given you to help you understand the result (this guidance works for any report you write).

  • This means, equations should be derived using FBDs, and plots should be well labelled and clear. Think about clarity of font, overlapping colors, legends etc.

    • Creating easy to read graphs and figures is a skill in itself, and worth putting the effort in now to learn. Your legend in matlab has a lot of flexibility that you might not immediately see. Make sure to check out the guide .
