Each process is defined by a delay, acquiring a lock, blinking the red and green LEDs (in sequence, not simultaneously), and then releasing the lock. This occurs for three iterations.

Since two identical processes are defined and only one can hold the lock at a given point in time, you should expect to see the red LED blink and then green LED blink 6 times in a row.
