On CMSX, you will be submitting separate files for each part of the lab (part1.s, part2.s, part3.s, part3ec.s). Since you will have overlapping definitions for most of your labels/functions, the best method to store your files together (without using multiple projects) is Exclusions . This tells your assembler to ignore certain files when creating builds of your project.
