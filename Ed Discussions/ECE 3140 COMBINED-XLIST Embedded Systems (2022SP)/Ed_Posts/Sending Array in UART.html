An array of what? There are many different ways to do this depending on your needs.

Let's say you have some array of uint8_t of unknown length (8 bit unsigned integers). Your data transmission could be sending the raw data of the entire array, ending with some type of terminator ( '\0' or other). When you receive the data, you can store a buffer of all the data received up to the terminator, and then split by byte to get your array back.
