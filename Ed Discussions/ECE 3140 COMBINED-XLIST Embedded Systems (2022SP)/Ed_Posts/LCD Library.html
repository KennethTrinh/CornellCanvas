Hi all,

I have a written a library to help students use the on-board LCD. The library has functions to write digits, decimal points, and the colon and additionally makes it easier to turn on or off individual segments for those of you who may want to write words.

The library is under the Final Project module on Canvas. Let me know if you have any questions/problems!

Good luck with the project,
