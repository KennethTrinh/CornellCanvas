You may use additional hardware such as Arduinos in your final project, but only the code for the KL46Z will be graded. While there is a possibility that we may offer some pin headers and soldering supplies so that students can attach components to your boards through jumpers and not by directly soldering to the board, we do not supply any additional hardware.
