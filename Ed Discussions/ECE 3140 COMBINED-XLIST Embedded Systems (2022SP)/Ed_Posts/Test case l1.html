The part of reader() where p1() is called does not require the lock r, so all the readers should be concurrent. This means that each reader should toggle the green LED at the same time, and since there are 3 readers, 3 simultaneous toggles appears like a single toggle, and the 5 flashes per process happen at the same time, appearing like 5 total flashes.
