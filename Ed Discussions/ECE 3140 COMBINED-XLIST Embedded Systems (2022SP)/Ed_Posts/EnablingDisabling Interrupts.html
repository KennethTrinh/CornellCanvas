Author: Aidan McNay (student)

Vote Count: 1

You should only use process_stack_free inside of process_select. This happens in the interrupt handler, where interrupts are already disabled! (There is the initial time we call process_select to find the first process that doesn't come in the interrupt handler. However, here we can note two things: 1) Your timer should be long enough that we don't get an interrupt here, and don't need to worry about an interrupt in process_stack_free 2) process_begin already enables global interrupts, so there isn't much you could change about it anyways :) This is more meant to prevent (at least I suspect) bugs in future cases where you are attempting to free processes while at risk of the timer running out, as if you get an interrupt while calling free(), weird/bad stuff happens)
