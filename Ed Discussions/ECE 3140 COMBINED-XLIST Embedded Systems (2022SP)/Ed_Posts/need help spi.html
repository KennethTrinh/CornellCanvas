If you are unable to debug/decipher the example code, you have two options: read the SPI chapter in the processor manual and set up the registers (similar process for figuring out GPIO in prior labs), or switch projects.

SPI (and other communication protocols) is very tricky when you are unable to visualize the transmitted signals. If you do not have access to electrical equipment like an oscilloscope, I would recommend going to a different project.
