Most likely you are pushing a different number of times than you are popping. Look at the stack as I did in the factorial demo to make sure that what you push onto the stack when you do PUSH {LR} is the same as when you do POP {PC}. My bet is that you accidentally push more or less times than you pop, and/or you push/pop out of order so the wrong value is loaded into the PC

If you are pushing LR then you'll need to pop that value into the PC (don't use BX LR)
