As written lock is safe (deadlock is not the issue). The statements in the lecture are about why you need the lock/unlock structure inside loop. The pattern looks a little odd , since when you break out of the inner loop nw=0 and the you grab the lock and check the exact same thing again in the outer loop. The issue is if you somehow did not grab the lock then two waiting processes that were spinning in the inner loop could break out of the inner loop, then both check the out loop, and then both proceed into the critical section, i.e. be unsafe.
