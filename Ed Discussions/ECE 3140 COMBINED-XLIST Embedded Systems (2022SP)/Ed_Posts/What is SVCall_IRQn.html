I'm not qualified enough to answer that question, but I think it is the same type as PIT_IRQn and kind of the Supervisor Call instruction's equivalent of PIT_IRQn . So it can be used in the NVIC_EnableIRQ() and NVIC_SetPriority() functions just as PIT_IRQn . Regardless, you don't have to use that in your code if you use the polling method for the issue discussed in #641 .
