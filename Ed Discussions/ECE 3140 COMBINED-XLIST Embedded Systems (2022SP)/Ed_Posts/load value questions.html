The whole goal of labs 3 through 5 is to support concurrent processes. If your load value is too high, there will be human visible differences between the processes. If your load value is too low, you spend too much time in context switches and you lose real execution time. Just as a hint, your load value should be closer in magnitude to lower value you tested than the higher value.
