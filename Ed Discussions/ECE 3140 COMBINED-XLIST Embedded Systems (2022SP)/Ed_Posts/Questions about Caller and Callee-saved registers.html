If R4-8, (R9), R10-11 are not used by the caller, why does the callee need to save them? Is it because another function could be called from here and the callee also becomes a caller in that case? Also if R0-3 are used by the caller, and are not used by the callee, does that mean they don't need to be caller-saved? I think for your first question, the caller expects R4 and beyond to remain the same before and after any function calls. For the second question, I think it's similar in the sense that R0-R3 are essentially free reign and the callee is allowed to modify them, which is why the caller needs to save them before making the function call. Not sure about the last question though.
