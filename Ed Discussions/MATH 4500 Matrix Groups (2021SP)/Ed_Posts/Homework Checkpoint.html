Let H be the sum of your highest six homework scores (out of homework assignments 1–8) and let P be your prelim score. (H is a number between 0 and 120, and P is a number between 0 and 50.) Let S=(5/12)×H+(1/2)×P be your "subtotal" score. Your subtotal S is a number between 0 and 75. The quartiles of the subtotal score distribution are Q1 61.7, Q2 64.3, Q3 69.5. Instructions for estimating your letter grade: if your subtotal S is less than 50, you earn a C+ or less; if S is between 50 and 65 you earn between a B- and a B+; if S is greater than 65, you earn an A- or more. This is a rough estimate only; your grade can still change between now and the end of the course.
