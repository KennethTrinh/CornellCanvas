Your matrix $A=\begin{pmatrix}0&1\\1&0\end{pmatrix}$ is diagonalizable: $A=SDS^{-1}$. Find $D$ and $S$ and then find $X$. You will need a complex $X$; a real $X$ won't work. Reason: if $A=e^X$, then $\det(A)=e^{\operatorname{tr}(X)}$, so if $X$ is real, then $\det(A)>0$. But your $A$ has determinant $-1$!
