If $\Pi\colon G\to\operatorname{GL}(V)$ is a representation and $W$ is an invariant subspace of $V$, then we get a representation $\Pi_W$ of $G$ on $W$ by defining $\Pi_W(A)w=\Pi(A)w$ for $w\in W$ and $A\in G$. This is well-defined because $\Pi(A)w\in W$ for all $w\in W$. We call the representation $\Pi_W\colon G\to\operatorname{GL}(W)$ the restriction of $\Pi$ to $W$.
