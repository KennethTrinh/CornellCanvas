The single bars refer to the quaternionic norm $|x|=\sqrt{x^*x}$.

This norm is related to the Hilbert-Schmidt norm by $\|x\|_{\text{HS}}=\sqrt2\,|x|$. The quaternionic norm is "better" in the sense that $|xy|=|x||y|$, whereas the Hilbert-Schmidt norm only satisfies $\|xy\|\le\|x\|\|y\|$.
