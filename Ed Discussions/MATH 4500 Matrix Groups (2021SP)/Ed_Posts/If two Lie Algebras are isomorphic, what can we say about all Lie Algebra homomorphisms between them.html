Title: If two Lie Algebras are isomorphic, what can we say about all Lie Algebra homomorphisms between them?

Author: Anonymous

Date: 2021-05-12T12:07:43.503683+10:00

Category: Homework

Vote Count: 0

Given two Lie Algebras $g_1$ and $g_2$. Suppose we know these are isomorphic. Now consider an arbitrary Lie Algebra homomorphism $\phi:g_1 \rightarrow g_2$ induced by some Lie Group homomorphism of the respective algebras. Can we say anything about this $\phi$?

Even though $\mathfrak{g}_1$ and $\mathfrak{g}_2$ are isomorphic, not every Lie algebra homomorphism $\phi\colon\mathfrak{g}_1\to\mathfrak{g}_2$ is an isomorphism. For instance, $\phi$ could be the zero map.
