You may use it, but you won't need it. In this course when I write $\|A\|$ I almost always mean the Hilbert-Schmidt norm $\|A\|_{\operatorname{HS}}$ of a matrix $A$. Any two norms on a finite-dimensional vector space are equivalent. It follows from $\|Ax\|\le\|A\|\|x\|$ that $$\|A\|_{\operatorname{operator}}\le\|A\|_{\operatorname{HS}}.$$ If you insist on using the operator norm in homework or exams, please make your intention clear, or else you may lose points.
