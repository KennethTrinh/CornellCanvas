A direct approach using formulas for determinants is likely to be clumsy. I suggest an indirect approach using some linear algebra. It's useful to distinguish two cases. Case 1. $\det(Z)=0$. Can you somehow show that $\det(M_Z)$ is also $0$? Case 2. $\det(Z)\ne0$. What are some facts you know about matrices with nonzero determinant?
