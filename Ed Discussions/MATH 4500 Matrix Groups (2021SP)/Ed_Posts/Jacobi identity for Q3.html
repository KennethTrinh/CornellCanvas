Hard to give a hint without giving the problem away.

One of the terms in the Jacobi identity is $[f,[g,h]]=[f,gh'-g'h]=f(gh'-g'h)'-f'(gh'-g'h)$. Use the product rule to simplify this. Then do the same for the other two terms in the Jacobi identity, and add up the results.
