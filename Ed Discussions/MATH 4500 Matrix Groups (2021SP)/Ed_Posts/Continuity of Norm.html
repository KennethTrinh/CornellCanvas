This can be seen as follows: it follows from the triangle inequality that

$$|\|x\|-\|a\||\le\|x-a\|$$

for all vectors $x$ and $a$ in a normed vector space. Therefore

$$0\le\lim_{x\to a}\bigl(|\|x\|-\|a\||\bigr)\le\lim_{x\to a}\|x-a\|=0,$$

i.e. $\lim_{x\to a}\|x\|=\|a\|$.
