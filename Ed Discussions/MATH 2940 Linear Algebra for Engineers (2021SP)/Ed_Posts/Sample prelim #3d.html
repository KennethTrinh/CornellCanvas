Why is [F(S)]B = [-S]B?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Phillip Jang (staff)

Vote Count: 0

$$F(S) = S^T = \begin{bmatrix}0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0\end{bmatrix}^T = \begin{bmatrix}0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0\end{bmatrix} = -S$$ Hence $[F(S)]_\mathcal{B} = [-S]_\mathcal{B}$.
