Author: Phillip Jang (staff)

Vote Count: 0

The order doesn't matter as long as the eigenvalues are in matching order with their corresponding eigenvectors. The $P^{-1}$ term in $PDP^{-1}$ cancels out your chosen order so that you get the same product.
