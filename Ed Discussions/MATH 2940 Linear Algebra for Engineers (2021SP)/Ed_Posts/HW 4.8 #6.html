To be completely accurate, you should probably examine the signals in more detail. For instance take $k=0$ and $k=1$ terms and put them as the columns of a $2\times 2$ matrix as: $$ \begin{bmatrix} 1&0\\ 0&5 \end{bmatrix} $$ to see that the signals are linearly independent.
