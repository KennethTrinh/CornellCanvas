What are the steps to calculate matrix for transformation F relative to B? Why does [F(P)]B = [P]B ? How is [P]B = [1; 0 ; 0 ; 0] calculated? Thanks

The basis is denoted $\mathcal{B}=\{P,Q,R,S\}$. $$F(P) = P^T = P = 1*P+0*Q+0*R+0*S$$ Hence we store the coefficients of $P,Q,R,S$ into the coordinate vector below: $$[F(P)]_\mathcal{B} = \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$$ Do the same with the other three basis matrices.
