The 1/8 comes from $1/det(P)$ in the formula of $P^{-1}$ for a 2x2 matrix. The matrix in 4e is $\lim_{k\rightarrow \infty}(\frac{1}{10}A)^k = P[\lim_{k\rightarrow \infty}(\frac{1}{10}D)^k]P^{-1}$.
