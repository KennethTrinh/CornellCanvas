Let $B = \begin{bmatrix}u_1 & u_2\end{bmatrix}$.

You need to find an orthonormal basis for the subspace of $\mathbb{R}^4$ not spanned by $u_1$ and $u_2$, namely $Col(B)^\perp = Nul(B^T) =$ Set of $\mathbb{R}^4$ vectors orthogonal to both $u_1$ and $u_2$.

Hence find the parametric vector form for the solution set of $B^T x = 0$ and orthonormalize the basis.
