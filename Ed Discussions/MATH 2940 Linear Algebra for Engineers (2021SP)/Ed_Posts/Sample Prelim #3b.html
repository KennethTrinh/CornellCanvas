$R$ and $S$ are the only matrices that affect the bottom left and top right, so we solve for $c_3$ and $c_4$ so that

$$\begin{bmatrix}0 & 4 \\ 8 & 0\end{bmatrix} = c_3R+c_4S = \begin{bmatrix}0 & c_3 \\ c_3 & 0\end{bmatrix}+ \begin{bmatrix}0 & c_4 \\ -c_4 & 0\end{bmatrix}$$

Do you see what to do now?
