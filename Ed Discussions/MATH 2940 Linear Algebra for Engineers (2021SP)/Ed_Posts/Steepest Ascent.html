I included this little fact to draw the analogy with maximizing a quadratic form over unit vectors. The vector c is given. In practice, it is the gradient vector for an objective function (think profit). The theorem says that to increase the objective you should move in the direction of the gradient vector - the idea underlying the most basic algorithm in Machine Learning. The proof is by the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality.
