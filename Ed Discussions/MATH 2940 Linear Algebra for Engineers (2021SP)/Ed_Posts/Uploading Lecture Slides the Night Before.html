I do understand that some students would like to study the slides in advance, or to annotate them as I teach. However, I prefer that students try to make the most of live lectures - watching and listening carefully to explanations of fresh material in real time, hopefully seeing it come to life, taking very few notes at first, and circling back to the slides and text later. Reading the slides in advance turns the lectures into tutorials or office hours, and turns the slides into a text: we have those resources already. My aim is different. I realize it's hard - hang in there! Adrian
