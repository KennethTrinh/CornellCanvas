Hello, we will use next Monday's session for you to present your first prototype. Each team will have time to go on stage and present to the entire class. Please arrive in class at least 10 minutes in advance as you probably need some more time to setup your system. Each team will have 13 minutes , including 9 minutes for presentation and 4 minutes for Q&A . This time restriction will be strictly enforced .

Please prepare slides to explain your design and plans and demonstrate your key functions live . In your presentation and slides, you need to show the following information:

  • (5 %) A clear description of your design goals for the project;

  • (5 %) A presentation of your overall design;

  • (50%) A demonstration of each key functional block of your prototype in action, including:

  • The target goal;

  • A description of the implementation and how it accomplishes.

  • The functional block of the prototype needs to be presented as a live demo

  • Pictures and video footage can be used to support the live demo, where applicable.

  • An analysis of discrepancies between the two and what should be done to address them;

  • (15 %) A plan of how the functional blocks will interact together to deliver the primary goal of your design. This description should include sketches that show how you would connect the key functional units or how they will be connected.

  • (10%) A plan of what needs to be done to complete the final prototype (including the extra features you would like to implement)

  • (10%) The quality of the presentation, such as the clarity of the presentation, how well the teams are prepared, and how the team answers the questions.

    • You will also need to submit your 2nd round part list to your TA on the same day.

    The order of the presentation will be announced after you arrive.

    Checklist :

  • Arrive at the classroom 10 minutes before class to setup your system.

  • Each team has 13 minutes (9min presentation + 4min Q&A).

  • Present your first prototype and demonstrate your key functions live .

  • Submit your slides to Canvas (due end of class).

  • Submit your 2nd round part list to your TA (due same day).

    • Please feel free to let us know if you have any questions.
