Title: Do not leave 3D printers until it finishes the first layer

Author: Cheng Zhang (admin)

Date: 2022-11-22T18:02:45.612813+11:00

Category: General

Vote Count: 0

As we discussed in class, the majority of 3D printing errors happen in the first 5-10 mins. I noticed most of the failed printing tasks did not finish the first layer.

Therefore, after you start printing your design, please stay there until it finishes the first layer ( usually about 3-5 mins). This would help save your time as well as resources.

Thank you.
