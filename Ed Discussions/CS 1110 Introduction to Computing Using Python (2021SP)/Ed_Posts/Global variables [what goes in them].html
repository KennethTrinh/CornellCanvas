No worries! But no, when you call a function from an import, you don't create the folder. (You do create the call frame for the function call. Call frames are the things with a box in the upper left and right corners. Folders are the things with a tab sticking up out of the upper left corner. ) Do consult the worked examples --- going through them and looking at the solutions we provided will make this all much clearer, I think!
