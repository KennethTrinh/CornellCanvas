The course website, under "Policies" and "Collaboration rules specific to CS 1110", has specific guidelines on what you can and cannot do with your classmates. If you choose not to register your friend as a partner on CMS, then you must abide by the course rules. This means, among other things, that you cannot share or receive portions of code, which could include sharing the general structure or how you approached a problem. To be safe, it's generally a good idea to register the person you're working with as a partner so the two of you can share as many ideas as you want.
