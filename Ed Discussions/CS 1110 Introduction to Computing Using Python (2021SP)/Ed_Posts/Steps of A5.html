By the way, one thing you're supposed to marvel at, in terms of the design of the code, is the fact that you can just swap module sd_utilities_dict.py for sd_utilities_list.py. This is modular design!

In other words, the showdown functionality doesn't need to know how the showdown utilities are implemented, just how they are supposed to be called and what they are supposed to do.
