In general, the differences between fall and spring call frame notation are, besides the "animation" aspect, that spring counts "else:" as an executed line and fall doesn't. So it seems like your first shred call frame needs to have the line 6 in the program counter as well. Other than that, looks good to my quick eyeball of your answer!
