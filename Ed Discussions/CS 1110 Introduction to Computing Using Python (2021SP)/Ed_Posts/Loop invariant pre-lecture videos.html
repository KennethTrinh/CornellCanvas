Loop invariants have not been covered in CS 1110 since Fall 2019 and you will cover them in enough detail to understand them when you are in CS 2110. That being said, if you want to learn about them you may find the following resources helpful:

  • The CS 1110 lecture on loop invariants: https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs1110/2019fa/lectures/page2/

  • The JavaHyperText entry on loop invariants: https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/JavaAndDS/loops/02looploopyq.pdf
