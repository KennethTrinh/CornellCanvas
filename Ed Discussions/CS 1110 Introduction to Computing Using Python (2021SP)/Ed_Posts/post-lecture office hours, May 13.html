General: how do you read a long question, like 2021Prelim 2, Q4. A:

  • Figure out whether "preamble" text is actually important, or just scene setting

  • Quickly skim the docstrings for any code that's given, to get an idea of what it does. Don't read the bodies yet! Just try to get an intuition for what's going on --- and how different functions/classes relate to each other (but NOT how they're implemented.)

  • When asked to write a function/method, read its docstring,

  • make sure you understand its inputs (usually there are preconditions, but there are certainly parameters) ..

  • and what it is supposed to do/return

  • Come up with high-ish level battle plan, or outline
