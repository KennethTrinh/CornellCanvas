Thank you for catching that! That seems to be a typo in the A6 handout -- a meeting time, in its "roster format", should always include an "am" or a "pm", according to Course's init function's precondition for the variable t that's supposed to represent the "roster format": 

t: string of the form (no beginning or trailing spaces)
    <days> <starthrs>:<startms><am or pm>-<endhrs>-<endms><am or pm>
   where <days> is a subsequence of "MTWRF"
   ***OR***
   the string "TBA"

Examples of valid values for t:
"MWF 10:10am-2:30pm"
"R 8:07am-11:00am"
"TBA"

So, please consider the example given in 8.3 of A6 handout to be “MWF 9:05am-9:55am”!
