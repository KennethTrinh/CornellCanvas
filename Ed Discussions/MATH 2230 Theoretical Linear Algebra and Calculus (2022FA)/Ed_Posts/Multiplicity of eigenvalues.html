There are two notions of multiplicity of an eigenvalue:

Geometric multiplicity =dimension of eigenspace;

Algebraic multiplicity =multiplicity as root of characteristic polynomial.

The geometric multiplicity of $\lambda$ is always less than or equal to the algebraic multiplicity.
