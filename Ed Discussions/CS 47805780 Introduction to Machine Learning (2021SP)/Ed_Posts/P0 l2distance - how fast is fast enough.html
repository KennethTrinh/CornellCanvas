It's expected that numpy computations should be much faster than the naive implementation. That being said, there could be a range of times based on your specific numpy implementation. 10x faster is a possibility, but you could also get numbers that would be over 1000x faster. As long as you used numpy somewhat efficiently, you shouldn't need to worry too much about it.
