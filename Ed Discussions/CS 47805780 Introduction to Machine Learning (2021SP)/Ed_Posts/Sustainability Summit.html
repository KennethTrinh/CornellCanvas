Celebrate Earth Week 2021 at Sustainability Summit ! Next week, we’re hosting four days of exciting speakers , networking sessions , and hands-on evening workshops that tackle the overarching theme of Sustainability! Notable events & opportunities: Hear from Marjorie Munneke, the Senior R&D Manager at Impossible Foods on the underlying synthetic biology that makes alternative proteins like the Impossible Burger , possible! Meet Sanchali Pal, a trailblazer in the world of ClimateTech and the Founder & CEO of Joro, the app that helps people take climate action that matters. Users connect their credit or debit cards to the app, and Joro measures their carbon footprint based on their spending habits. Meet three Cornell professors and learn about their sustainability research and what they’re looking for in undergrad research assistants through this “Get Involved with Sustainability Research” networking session . Curious about your Carbon Footprint? Join us for the Carbon Calculators Workshop co-presented Cornell Sustainability Consultants to learn more about your personal impact on the world. Sustainability Summit will go out with a BANG! We’re hosting Adam Met, the A in AJR who also serves as the Executive Director for Sustainable Partners, Inc. Hear about how his life as part of AJR, coupled with his studies in Philosophy, Constitutional Religious Law, and Human Rights from Columbia University and NYU, led him to where he is today.
