$w^* \cdot w^{t+1} \ge w^* \cdot w^{t} + \gamma$ $w^* \cdot w^{t} \ge w^* \cdot w^{t-1} + \gamma$ $w^* \cdot w^{t-1} \ge w^* \cdot w^{t-2} + \gamma$ ... $w^* \cdot w^{1} \ge w^* \cdot w^{0} + \gamma$ So we get $$w^* \cdot w^{t+1} \ge (w^* \cdot w^{t}) + \gamma \ge (w^* \cdot w^{t-1} + \gamma) + \gamma \ge ((w^* \cdot w^{t-2} + \gamma) + \gamma) + \gamma$$ and so on which collapses to the final statement.
