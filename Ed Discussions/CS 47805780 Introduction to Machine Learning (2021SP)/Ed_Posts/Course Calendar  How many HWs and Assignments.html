Hi! According to the lecture slides from the Kick-off session (see under modules in canvas), there are at most 5 homeworks and 5 projects. Each is due a week later. Take the total number of weeks in a semester (~14 weeks) and divide by 5 taking into account 1 week per assignment to submit, so approximately every 2 weeks there is homework and a project.
