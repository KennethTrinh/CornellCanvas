We just released the instructions and the data for the final project on Canvas. Everything is explained in the PDF that you find there, but here are the key points and an immediate action item. The final project is due on Thursday, May 13, and you can do the project in groups of 1-3 students. Every project group will have a mentor, and thus we need to know the groups. There please declare your group (even if you do it as a "group" of one person) by Monday, May 3 via Canvas. In particular, please do the following to declare your group: Go onto Canvas https://canvas.cornell.edu/courses/27847 Click on people in the navigation toolbar. Click “Final Project Groups’ in the top tabs click “+Group” in the top right corner. Name your group. Be sure it is the same name on Kaggle and everywhere else as well. Invite your team members to join. Submit. If you have any questions about this process, please email Lacy Jordaens at lsl92@cornell.edu. Looking forward to seeing your projects! Cheers Thorsten
