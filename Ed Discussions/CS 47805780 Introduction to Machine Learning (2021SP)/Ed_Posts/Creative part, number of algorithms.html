The two algorithm requirement is just for the baseline solution (i.e. not necessary for the creative part, but could be useful).

From the handout, in the section describing train_creative_t_values:

This is a lot of data so feel to use any number of these columns to improve your prediction.

You do not need to produce two separate sets of predictions based on separate training for each of those csvs. The data in train_creative_t_values is not strictly necessary for producing your creative result, but could definitely improve your model.
