Ah! Yep, this question is a little bit tricky to think about, even after solidly understanding VC dimension. One tip would be to take a look at the VC dimension of a rectangle (non-rotatable!) These notes are really nice: https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs683/2008sp/lecture%20notes/683notes_0428.pdf. This shows a way to shatter 4 points with rectangles. Then, think about nesting the rectangles like in the donut, and shattering all the combined points. Does it work?
