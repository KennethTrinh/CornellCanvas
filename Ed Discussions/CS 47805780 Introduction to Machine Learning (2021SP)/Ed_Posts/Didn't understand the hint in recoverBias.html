You should find the alpha that is furthest away from both $0$ and $C$, call it $\alpha_i$, to eventually get the most stable hyperplane bias. Then you take the $y_i$ corresponding to $\alpha_i$. Finally, you want to output bias, so use this information to solve for bias and have your function output that value.
