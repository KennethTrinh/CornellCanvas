The value you choose for the SGD update must be an unbiased estimator of the gradient. The gradient of of the loss function at timestep $t$ is $dL(x^{(t)}) = -2$ (take derivative of $L$ w.r.t. $x$ and plug in $-1$). So, we need $E[g^{(t)}] = -2$ $$E[g^{(t)}] = 0.5 * -3 + 0.5 * -1 = -2$$. Therefore it's a valid estimator.
