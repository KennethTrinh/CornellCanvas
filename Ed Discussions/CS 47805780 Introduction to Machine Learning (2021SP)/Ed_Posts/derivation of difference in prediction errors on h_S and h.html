Symbolically all you're doing is this: $$x - y = (x - z) + (z - w) + (w - y)$$ The $z$ and $w$ will cancel no matter what value you substitue in for them (in this case it's $z = err_{S}(h_{S})$ and $w = err_{S}(h^\star)$)
