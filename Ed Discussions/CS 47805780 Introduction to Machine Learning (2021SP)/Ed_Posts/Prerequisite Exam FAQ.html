Some important information for the prereq exam (Check back for updates that will be edited into this post!)

  • Gradescope submissions are now open. Check Canvas for the link.

  • The .tex file is now available for typesetting your solutions. Again, available on Canvas -- you will be linked to Overleaf; copy the template and fill in your answers where indicated.
