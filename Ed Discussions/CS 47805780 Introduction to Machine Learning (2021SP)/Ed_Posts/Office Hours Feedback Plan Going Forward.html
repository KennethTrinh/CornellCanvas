Greetings Everyone, Hope you're all doing well. We realize nowadays can be a very stressful time, and not managing to get help during office hours can aggravate that. Therefore, going forward, there are two things to note that we believe will help make OH a better experience. First, we will try our best to set a fairly strict time limit of 12-min per person, to reach more people than we are currently. Second, we ask that you please write your topic/concern in as much detail as you can. We strongly recommend against using filler for the topic just because then we can group individual questions together and avoid the bottleneck of breakout rooms. Best and have a good Friday, 4780 Staff
