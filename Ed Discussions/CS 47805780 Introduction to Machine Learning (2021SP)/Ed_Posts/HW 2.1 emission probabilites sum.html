They don't sum to 1 because we are only given a subset of the possible words in the vocabulary. I.e. given that a word $w_i$ is a verb, it could also be emitted as some other word that isn't included in "Students want a break". If we were given all of the possible words in the vocabulary, it would likely be the case that columns sum to 1. However, rows should not sum to 1, because two probabilities like $\Pr(X=Students|Y=ART)$ and $\Pr(X=Students|Y=V)$ are dependent on different conditions of $Y$, and thus aren't part of the same "situation", in a sense.
