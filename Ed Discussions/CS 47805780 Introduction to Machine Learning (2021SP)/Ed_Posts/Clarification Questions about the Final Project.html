To clarify, "next_week_increase_decrease" refers to the date exactly 1 week in the future. For line 23 4/5/2020 it is referring to 4/12/2020 which has a decrease in hospitalizations. year_week is just an additional variable that came in the dataset and in fact provides less specific information than the actual date, so you can choose whether or not to use it.
