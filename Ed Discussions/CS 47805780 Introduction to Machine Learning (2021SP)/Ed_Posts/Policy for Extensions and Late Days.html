According to the intro slides, "everybody has 7 “free” late days for assignments. They mitigate any delays that are outside your control (e.g. sickness, network outage, weather, broken computer, sick TAs, tardy group mates). Use them wisely. Lowest assignment grade will be replaced by second-lowest assignment grade." You get 7 free late days for projects too (separately from HWs, as far as I understand). Lowest grade for projects is also replaced by 2nd lowest.
