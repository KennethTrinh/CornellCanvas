Author: Michael Noor (staff)

For each x, b is just an additional 1 in the last term. You should treat it like any other x_i in updating perceptron.

For example, if a point we had already was x_i=(x_i0, x_i1, ..., x_in) we append a 1 to the end, so now x_i=(x_i0, x_i1, ..., x_in, 1)
