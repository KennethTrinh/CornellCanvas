{size of hypothesis space H} * 2 * exp(-(m*epsilon^2)/2^2) ?

Hoeffding's inequality normally has the term $2e^{-2m\epsilon^{2}}$ on the RHS. So the $2$ cancels with the $4$ in the denominator (taken from the wiki ).
