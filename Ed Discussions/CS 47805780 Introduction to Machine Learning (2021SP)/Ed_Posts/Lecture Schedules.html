The asynchronous format allows lots of flexibility in when you complete lectures and work. I'd suggest starting early on both projects and homework to take full advantage of office hours (which are sometimes cramped around deadlines). Additionally, make sure to watch the lectures thoroughly before discussion section to get the most value out of the problems. Otherwise, when you complete the lectures and work is up to you.
