Hi everyone, Project 2 is now released on Vocareum. You can also find it on Canvas/Modules/Project 2, which will direct you to Vocareum. Given the wellness days next week, we are extending the deadline for Project 2 to Mar 16, 11:59 PM, with 3 slip days allowed; it is an individual project. This project consists of 2 parts. The first part is implementing a perceptron, and an auto grader will be used to grade your code like project 1. The second part is a competition. You will get full scores on this part if your classifier beats our baseline implementation. When submitting your solution, the competition_ungraded in the report is your accuracy on the testing set. If you have any questions, feel free to reply to this post or create a new question on Ed discussion. Good Luck! Clarifications: For perceptron implementation, by iteration we mean one round of updates on every training data. Then you may reshuffle the data and start the next round. No need to worry about this if you already passed the autograder tests. For the competition part, our baseline accuracy is 97% , you will get the 5 points if your perceptron gives higher accuracies. You may want to tune the feature dimensions and add cross-validation.
