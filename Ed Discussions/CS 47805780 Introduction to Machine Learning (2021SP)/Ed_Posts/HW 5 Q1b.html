Author: Scott Bass (student)

You're missing the multiplication by $P(Y=-1)$ and $P(Y=1)$:

$$P(X_{i} = z) = P(X_{i} = z | Y = -1)P(Y = -1) + P(X_{i} = z | Y = 1)P(Y = 1)$$

So $P(X_{i} = 0) = 0.4 \times 0.3 + 0.6 \times 0.7 = 0.54$
