Author: Justin Lee (staff)

Well each feature in the training set is a bag of letters so to say. You are essentially finding the $$P(X_{i} \geq 1 | y =\pm 1)$$. This is equivalent to finding $$P(X_{i} = x| y =\pm 1)$$ for a letter x
