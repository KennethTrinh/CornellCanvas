For the first part, I would say that even if the form of the partial derivative is unfamiliar, maybe we can reason about it? That is to say, how could you perform ∂Ul/∂L​ using chain rule in terms of things you know?

As for the second part, I would say in general that you should always simplify the multiplication in your chain rule to quantities involving that layer and the following layers.
