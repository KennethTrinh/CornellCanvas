I agree! That rule does not make much sense, since it gives more weight to the nearest neighbors that are further away. Note that I have the same formula in my lecture slides, but with a big difference. I use a similarity measure K to determine the k nearest neighbors, not a distance function $\rho$. A similarity measure becomes larger the more similar the two features vectors are, which makes the weighting formula on my slides more sensible.
