The non-commutative operation $X\times Y$ only works if given $X$'s dimensions are $(n,d)$ and $Y$'s dimensions are $(d,m)$ for some arbitrary $n,d,m \in \mathbb{Z}^+$. I hope this clears up the error you are having, otherwise please feel free to follow up.
