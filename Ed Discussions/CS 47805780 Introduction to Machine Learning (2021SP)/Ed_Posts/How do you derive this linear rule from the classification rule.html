Starting from the second to last line we can form the ratio:

$$\frac{exp(-\frac{1}{2}||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{+}||^{2})P(Y=+1)}{exp(-\frac{1}{2}||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{-}||^{2})P(Y=-1)} = exp(-\frac{1}{2}(||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{+}||^{2} - ||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{-}||^{2}))\frac{P(Y=+1)}{P(Y=-1)}$$

Taking the log of this ratio turns the argmax into sign:

$$sign(-\frac{1}{2}(||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{+}||^{2} - ||\vec{x}-\vec{\mu}_{-}||^{2})+\ln\frac{P(Y=+1)}{P(Y=-1)})$$

Focusing on the first term we can expand it and cancel terms:

$$-\frac{1}{2}(\cancel{\vec{x} \cdot \vec{x}} - 2\vec{\mu}_{+} \cdot \vec{x} + \vec{\mu}_{+} \cdot \vec{\mu}_{+} -

\cancel{\vec{x} \cdot \vec{x}} + 2\vec{\mu}_{-} \cdot \vec{x} - \vec{\mu}_{-} \cdot \vec{\mu}_{-}

)$$

$$= \vec{\mu}_{+} \cdot \vec{x} - \vec{\mu}_{-} \cdot \vec{x} - \frac{1}{2} (\vec{\mu}_{+} \cdot \vec{\mu}_{+} - \vec{\mu}_{-} \cdot \vec{\mu}_{-})$$

$$= (\vec{\mu}_{+} - \vec{\mu}_{-}) \cdot \vec{x} - \frac{1}{2} (\vec{\mu}_{+} + \vec{\mu}_{-}) \cdot (\vec{\mu}_{+} - \vec{\mu}_{-})$$

Final expression:

$$sign((\vec{\mu}_{+} - \vec{\mu}_{-}) \cdot \vec{x} + (\ln\frac{P(Y=+1)}{P(Y=-1)} - \frac{1}{2} (\vec{\mu}_{+} + \vec{\mu}_{-}) \cdot (\vec{\mu}_{+} - \vec{\mu}_{-})))$$
