You are asked to give a single neural network that takes in both x1 and x2 and gives two 1D outputs y1 and y2, using a single hidden layer. CORRECTION: You are not limited to just using ReLU nodes in your hidden layer, you are just limited to at most 4 ReLU nodes in the hidden layer. You may include in your hidden layer, for example, nodes that simply perform the linear combination and transfer that value to the output nodes.
