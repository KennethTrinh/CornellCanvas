IIRC, a kernel is just the center (functional or geographic) of something. For example, the kernel of an operating system is functionally the core of the OS, the kernel of a function is kinda the center I guess, and the ML kernel is really the basis of the classifier. Plus, the popcorn kernel is the center of the popcorn.
