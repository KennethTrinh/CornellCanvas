    I will be happy to help here:

  • After presenting your initial design, your TA will provide feedback that you will then integrate into your final design proposal. It is most likely that the two designs will be quite different;

  • AN ordered list will work to express the priority of each features;

  • The goal of the previous work search is to understand how people have implemented your ideas in the past. The wider the search the more likely you will find an interesting solution to solve your problem. So at this point, it is important not to focus on a specific mechanism.
