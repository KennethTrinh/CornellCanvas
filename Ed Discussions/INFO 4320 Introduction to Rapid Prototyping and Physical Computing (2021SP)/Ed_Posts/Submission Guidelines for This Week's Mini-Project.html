Please read carefully, especially if you have already submitted the assignment.

Use the following submission guidelines for this week's mini-project:

  • Teapot

  • Submit an .f3d file with the format "lastname_firstname_teapot"

  • Ex: "shi_felix_teapot"

  • Submit a screenshot of the home view

  • Crank-Piston Assembly

  • Submit an .f3d file with the format "lastname_firstname_assembly"

  • Ex: "shi_felix_assembly"

  • In the comments of your submission, add a link that goes to a YouTube video of the mechanism in motion. Do not submit a Google Drive link, .mov, or .mp4 file . Downloading these files takes a lot of time. You can animate the crank by right-clicking on the revolute joint that fixes your crank to the box and selecting "Animate Model." Here is an example: https://youtu.be/p8C3qCWlszU

    • Do not submit these in a .zip file. It adds an extra step on our end.

    If you have already submitted the assignment, please resubmit your files in this format.

    I apologize for sending this out a day before the due date, especially because over half of you have submitted already. However, these little steps on your end save hours on our end.

    Grades for the duck and envelope will be released tomorrow.

    Thank you!
