Hi Everyone,

Our first class is around the corner (Feb 8th). In the first class, we will have several discussion-based teaching activities. One of them requires the use of external materials.

TAs have put all the needed materials for this Monday class into bags, and placed them in the lobby of Gates Hall. . We may need to clean the area before Monday. So please pick them up during the weekends.

Here are the steps that you need to complete for this self-service pickup procedure:

1. Book a pick-up slot on the sheets by filling your name in the column. The slot is first come first serve: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZXrofwWc5vdaMbEWQyyUs8pNIGwXP2RjVST7zc8Z-hk/edit?usp=sharing

2. Show up on time to pick one bag, following all required health protocols, including, but not limited to wearing facemasks and keep social distancing.

If you see another student is picking the package, please do not enter the area until that student complete the procedure.

If you are late for your reserved slot, please book another slot.

Once you take your bag, PLEASE LEAVE IMMEDIATELY. DO NOT STAY IN THE AREA.

3. After taking the bag, filling your name in the second column to confirm: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZXrofwWc5vdaMbEWQyyUs8pNIGwXP2RjVST7zc8Z-hk/edit?usp=sharing

Please let us know if you have any questions. Looking forward to meeting you all on Monday.
