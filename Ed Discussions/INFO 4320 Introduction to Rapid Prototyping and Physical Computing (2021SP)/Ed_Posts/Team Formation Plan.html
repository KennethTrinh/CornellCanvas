Hi Everyone,

Some of you have not formed a team yet. We plan to use part of Wed's class to help you find a team. Here is the plan.

_________________________________________

If you have formed a team (3 or 4 students), please sign up for a weekly meeting with one of the 6 TAs (we just had two more TA, Alex and Isaac) using this google Sheets: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1m79HSQhCC9OiwnHVCbE13TwYRN7u8iTbSn3bAHIP0q4/edit?usp=sharing

Please note that we may switch teams between TAs to balance the workload later.

________________________________________

If you are looking for a team, here is what you can do:

1. Check ideas on Miro : We have sorted all ideas on Miro and converged 13 topic areas, Food machine, Household Automation, Health, Music, Paint/Print, Pets, Fashion$Clothing, Toys, Wearables, Games/VR, Energy/Solar, Walking Robots, Agriculture.

2. We created 13 channels on Discord for these 13 topics respectively. Each channel has two sub-channels for video/audio chat and text communication respectively.

3. During tomorrow's class, we will leave around 30 mins such that you can go into the channels you are interested in to have direct video/audio chat with other students who share similar interests.

4. After class, you can post your ideas and team seeking information on the text sub-channel under each topic.

5. If you do not have preferred topics or you are broadly looking for teammates, you can post information on #finding-a-team channel on Discord, or Ed Discussion.

For instance, if I want to work on a project related to Pets, I would 1) first check out all ideas on the Pet Frame on Miro . 2) Then I will talk to others using the audio/video sub-channel under Pet Channel on Discord during the class. 3) after class, I can post my ideas about Pets on the text sub-channel under Pet channel or #finding-a-team channel, or Ed Discussion.

Once you form a team, please sign up a meeting with TA ASAP as a way of declaring teams.

Please let us know if you have any questions. See you all tomorrow.
