We got some questions on how to draw the schematic for your design project (RGB LED). You can either 1) draw it by hand on paper, or 2) using software platforms to draw the schematic. Both would satisfy the requirements. Here are some of the software/platforms which you can draw schematics: 1. https://fritzing.org/download/ 2. https://www.tinkercad.com/
