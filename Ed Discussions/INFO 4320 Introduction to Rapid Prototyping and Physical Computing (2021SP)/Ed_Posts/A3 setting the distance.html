Yes, adjust the potentiometer to change the target distance and demonstrate that your code works for this new distance. Try not to put the new distance too close (<5 cm) together to the old one.

You can adjust the distance by either moving the sensor toward your object or moving your object toward the sensor. We want to see the LED changing in brightness as it gets closer/farther.
