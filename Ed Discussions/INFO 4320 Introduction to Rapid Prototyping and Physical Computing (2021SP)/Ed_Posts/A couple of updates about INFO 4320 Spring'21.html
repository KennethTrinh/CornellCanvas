As we are all preparing for the new semester, we wanted to provide some important information about INFO 4320 Spring'21: Because of the pandemic, the course will be taught online this semester. We will be using Zoom during the lecture part of each classes and Discord during the more interactive segments of the class; You will still have access to hardware resources (Laser cutter, 3D printers) to conduct your projects, but you should expect more constrains on accessing these resources and working in group because of the current pandemic; We are looking into providing each student with a document camera to help support hardware debugging. To better understand individual needs we would like you to complete this survey as soon as possible; As in the past, all students will be registered at once after having settled on a group, a project topic and a weekly meeting time with one of our TAs. This process should take place in the second week of the semester; We will of course provide far more details during our first lecture on Monday. In the meantime do not hesitate to contact us using this platform if you have any questions. Team INFO 4320
