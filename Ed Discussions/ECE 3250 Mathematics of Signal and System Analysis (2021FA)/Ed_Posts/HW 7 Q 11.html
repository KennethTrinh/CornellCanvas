Yup. That's a calculus thing, and here's the general form of it:

$\frac{d}{d \Omega} \int _ a ^ b f ( t , \Omega ) d t = \int _ a ^ b \frac{\partial{f}}{\partial{\Omega}} \, d t$.

I think people call it the Leibniz Rule.
