More complicated than that. If $x$ is 1 from 0 to 1 and zero otherwise, then ${\rm Shift} _ 1 ( x )$ is 1 from 1 to 2, and ${\rm Scale} _ 2 {\rm Shift} _ 1 ( x )$ is 1 from $1 / 2$ to 1.
