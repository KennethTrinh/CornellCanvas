What I think you're driving at is correct, but your expression mixes whole-signal notation with value-at-time-$n$ notation. Here are two equivalent statements, one using each kind of notation: ${\rm Shift} _ {k _ o} ( h _ 1 * x ) = h _ 1 * {\rm Shift} _ {k _ o} ( x )$. ${\rm Shift} _ {k _ o} ( h _ 1 * x ) ( n ) = h _ 1 * {\rm Shift} _ {k _ o} ( x ) ( n )$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
