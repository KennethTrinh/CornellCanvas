If you are finite duration in the time domain, then you can't have bandlimiitedness in the frequency domain. One example that I just keep in mind is the "boxcar" or the pulse function in the time domain, whose fourier spectrum is a sinc which clearly isn't necessarily ceterered around certain frequencies and applies over every frequency value possible.
