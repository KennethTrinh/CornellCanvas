Hard to see exactly what you're asking, but we want to allow our LTI systems to include the identity system and the pure $t _ 1$-shift system, which is why our standing assumption allows a system to operate on an input by = convolving the input with a decent signal $h _ 0$, and then = adding on some shifts of the input That's just the general form of LTI systems we consider in this course. When write out the system's impulse response, the shift terms, if any, contribute impulses.
