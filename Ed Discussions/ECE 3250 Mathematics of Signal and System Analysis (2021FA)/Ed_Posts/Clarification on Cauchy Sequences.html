The partial sums $s _ n = \sum _ {m = 1} ^ n \frac{1}{m}$ don't converge --- if they did, the series $\sum _ {n = 1} ^ \infty \frac{1}{n}$ wold converge --- that's the definition of series convergence. I think you might be conflating sequences and series; A sequence $\{ c _ n \}$ is Cauchy when its terms get closer and closer together. Turns out that every Cauchy sequence of real or complex numbers converges.
