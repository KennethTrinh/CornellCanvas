The inner product we use on $X _ {T _ o}$ is

$\langle x , y \rangle = \frac{1}{T _ o} \int _ {T _ o} x ( t ) \overline{y ( t )} d t$,

where $\int _ {T _ o}$ means integral over any interval of length $T _ o$.
