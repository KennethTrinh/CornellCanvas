If I understand your question, it's because u(t-tau) is 1 while tau <= t, and zero for tau > t. u is a function of tau because that's the integration variable. Any unit step for a negative function variable equals 1 up until its "argument" is zero, beyond which it is 0. Like u(2-t) is 1 for t <= 2, and 0 for t>2.
