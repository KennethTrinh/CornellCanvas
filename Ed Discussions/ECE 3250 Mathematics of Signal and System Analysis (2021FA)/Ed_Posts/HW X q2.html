It turns out that for every $k$, the Haar wavelets $\psi _ {n , m }$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$ and all $m < k$ form a complete orthonormal set in $V _ k$. We didn't penetrate the theory deeply enough to get to that. For projection onto the $V _ k$, please use the scaled and shifted scaling function.
