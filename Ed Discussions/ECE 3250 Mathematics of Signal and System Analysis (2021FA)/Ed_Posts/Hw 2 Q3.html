Both, kind of, but not totally. First show that the recursion holds. Then, assuming that $\{ q _ n \}$ converges, show that it converges to the golden ratio --- the result of (b) helps. The key here: we assume $\{ q _ n \}$ converges, but don't prove it --- all we do is find out what it converges to given that it converges.
