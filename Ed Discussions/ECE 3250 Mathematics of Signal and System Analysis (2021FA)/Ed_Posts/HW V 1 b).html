Say you found the that $\| S ( x ) - \overline{y} \| _ \infty \leq M$ for some $M$ that depends on $\| d \| _ \infty$, like for example $M = 17 \| d \| _ \infty$. Then given $\epsilon$ you want to find some $d$ with $\| d \| _ \infty = \epsilon$, so that $\| S ( x ) - \overline{y} \| = 17 \epsilon$ when $x = \overline{x} + d$.
