For a geometric series $\sum _ {n = 0} ^ \infty \gamma ^ n$ to converge, you need $| \gamma | < 1$. If you set up the DTFT equation for that signal, you get a geometric series with $\gamma = 3 e ^ {- j \omega}$, which has magnitude 3.
