One idea: plug in $t = 0$ and the formula for $p _ a$ and you end up with

$\frac{1}{a T} \int _ {- T} ^ 0 p _ a ( \tau ) d \tau$,

which, for $a$ small enough (specifically, $a < T / 2$) equals

$\frac{1}{a T} \int _ {- a / 2} ^ 0 1 d \tau = \frac{1}{a T} ( a / 2 ) = \frac{1}{2 T}$.

You can calculate the $t = T$ case similarly.
