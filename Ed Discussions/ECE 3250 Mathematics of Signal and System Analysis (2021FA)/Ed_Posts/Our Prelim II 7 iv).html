If it's periodic and not the zero signal, it's magnitude-square integral over any period is positive, say $c _ o$, so its magnitude squared integral over all time is the sum of infinitely many $c _ o$'s, hence not finite. Thus if it's in $L ^2$, it can't be nonzero and periodic.
