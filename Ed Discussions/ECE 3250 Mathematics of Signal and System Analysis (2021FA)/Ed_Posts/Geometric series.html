Author: David Delchamps (admin)

You can usually simplify, but the problem doesn't demand that you do. Example:

$\sum _ {k = 1} ^ \infty \gamma ^ k = \frac{1}{1 - \gamma} - 1 = \frac{\gamma}{1 - \gamma}$

when $| \gamma | < 1$.
