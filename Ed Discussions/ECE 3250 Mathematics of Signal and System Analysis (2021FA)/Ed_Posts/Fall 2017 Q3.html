Why is (ii) true? I am really confused about how to calculate this.

Author: Raleigh Clemens (student)

Vote Count: 2

Given $p<q$, $p \in \mathbb{Z}_q^*$. $\langle p^q\rangle _q = \langle \langle p \rangle _q * \langle p^{q-1} \rangle _q \rangle _q$, where the second term is $1$ by Fermat's Little Theorem. So we just have $\langle \langle p \rangle _q \rangle _q$ left, which is $p$.
