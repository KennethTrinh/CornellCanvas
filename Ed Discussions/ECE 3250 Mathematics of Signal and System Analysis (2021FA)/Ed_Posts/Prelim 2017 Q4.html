The fundamental period of $x$ is 17, so all the periods of $x$ are all the integer multiples of 17, including 51. And BTW 51 is an integer multiple of $17/3$. Remember, the "periods of $x$" aren't "the fundamental periods of $x$'s Fourier series's terms."
