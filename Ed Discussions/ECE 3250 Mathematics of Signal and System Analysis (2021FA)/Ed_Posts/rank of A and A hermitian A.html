$A ^ H A$ and $A$ both have $n$ columns and their nullspaces are exactly the same. Since rank is $n$ - dimension of the nullspace, their ranks are the same.

Proof in monograph. I'll put it here when back at a computer. Did you look in the monograph before posing these questions?

Now back at a computer:

$A v = 0$ clearly implies $A ^ H A v = 0$.

$A ^ H A v = 0$ implies $v ^ H A ^ H A v = 0$ implies $\left \| A v \right \| ^ 2 = 0$ implies $A v = 0$.

Conclusion: $A v = 0$ if and only if $A ^ H A v = 0$.
