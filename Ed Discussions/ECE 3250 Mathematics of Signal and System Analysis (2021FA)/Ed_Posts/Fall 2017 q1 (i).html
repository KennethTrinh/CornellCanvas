We saw this in class. $f: \mathbb{N} \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}$ defined by

$f ( n ) = \left \{ \begin{array}{cl} n / 2 & {\rm when} \; n \; {\rm is \; even} \\ - ( n + 1 ) / 2 & {\rm when} \; n \; {\rm is \; odd} \; .\end{array} \right .$
