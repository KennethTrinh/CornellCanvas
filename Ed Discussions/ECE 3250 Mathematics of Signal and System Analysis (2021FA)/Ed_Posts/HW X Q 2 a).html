If $W$ is a subspace and $\mathcal{W}$ is a finite or countably infinite complete orthonormal set in $W$, then

${\rm Proj} ( x \mid W ) = \sum _ {w \in \mathcal{W}} \langle x , w \rangle \, w$.

You want to apply this here to $W = V _ 0$, and $\mathcal{W} = \{ \phi _ {n , 0} : n \in \mathbb{Z} \}$. Turns out that few of the inner products $\langle x , \phi _ {n , 0} \rangle$ are nonzero.
