For Early Admits, I reach out to them during their first week or two of the semester that they actually enroll in the MEng program and send a copy of their original Early Admit Petition, along with a blank copy of the form.

I ask them to review it, and if there are changes (there almost always are to some degree), I ask to have them submit an updated copy, so I know for certain what they took in their Early ADmit semester. Then, I will know what to reference when checking if they've met degree requirements, when it comes to graduation.

If you have more specific questions, please let me know - sec36@cornell.edu
