  • ECE 3250 will change this coming fall to make up for the disappearance of ECE 2200.

  • The new course title is Signals and Systems .

  • Neither the Courses of Study catalogue nor the Class Roster reflects the changes yet.

  • That said, the syllabus for the new version is available through the Class Roster --- just go here https://classes.cornell.edu/browse/roster/FA22/class/ECE/3250 and access the syllabus through the link at the top of Enrollment Information.
