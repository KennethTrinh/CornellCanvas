Author: Scott Coldren (admin)

To the best of my knowledge, there is nothing preventing a student from serving as an MEng GTRS (Graduate Teaching Research Specialist - they're no longer called "TA") for more than one course. ECE would very likely not appoint one student to two separate courses. There is no tuition or health insurance support offered.
