Dave is partially correct. The deadline to apply for consideration is 01/15/2022 for Fall 2023 for the Early Admit option. If you're going to still be attempting to figure out your course situation, it will be too late for you to apply. In order to apply for and be considered for the Early Admit option, you will have to submit both an ECE Undergraduate Checklist, as well as a completed Early Admit Petition (these are both in addition to the actual online application itself). If your Checklist (i.e. your graduation plan) is not approved, meaning you have more than 8 credit hours left in your undergraduate degree requirements, you cannot be considered for Early Admit.
