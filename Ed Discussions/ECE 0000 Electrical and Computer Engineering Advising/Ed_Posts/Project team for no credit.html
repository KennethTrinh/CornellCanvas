If you are referring to the 20 credit limit, the Registrar seems like they will be automatically approving overage for project teams (given that you are not also pursuing research or TA credit). Also note that project teams under ENGRG 1400/MAE 1900/MAE 4900 are now S/U exclusive starting FA22.

You could also talk to the team and see if they offer a volunteer status. If this is your first semester participating on a project team, you must enroll in ENGRG 1400 (project team onboarding). Failure to do so may result in removal from the team.
