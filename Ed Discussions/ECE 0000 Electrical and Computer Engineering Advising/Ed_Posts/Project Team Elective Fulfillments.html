Can we have credit from project teams count towards both an advisor-approved elective as well as an outside technical elective? With that, is there a limit to the number of semesters of project team credit that can be applied towards these elective requirements?

Author: David Delchamps (admin)

Vote Count: 0

You can count up to 3 credits of project-team toward OTE. You can count as many credits as you want toward Advisor-approved Elective. You just can't count the same credit hours twice. Typically you'll count one semester toward OTE and two others toward AAE.
