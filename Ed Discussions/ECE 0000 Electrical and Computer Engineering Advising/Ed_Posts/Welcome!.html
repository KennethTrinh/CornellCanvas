Welcome to the Ed Discussion for the ECE undergraduate program. Ask away! There are copious categories to facilitate searching. As with any Ed Discussion:

  • Try searching before you post to see if your question has already been asked.

  • "Heart" questions and answers you find useful.

  • Answer questions you feel confident answering.

  • Keep it constructive and professional. If you need to vent to someone with the power the change things, I am happy to lend an ear in person or over the phone.

    • For more information on Ed Discussion, you can refer to the Quick Start Guide .
