Cornell Maker Club is recruiting this spring! Our Executive board is seeking (Non Current) members who are particularly interested in business,management, and web design. It is a great leadership opportunity to gain hands-on experience working with lab equipment (i.e. laser cutter, 3D printers, etc.), coordinating with ECE and College of Engineering admins to organize CMC events (Make-a-thon), Representing CMC/ECE at university sponsored events (i.e. Homecoming, Club Fair, ECE Day ), administering training to new lab members, and managing CMC website. The application is quick and easy, and all skill levels are welcomed. Apply at: https://forms.gle/KYrSAQcYDHNZWDVe9 APPLICATIONS DUE: 03/06/22 Become a CMC member: https://makerclub.ece.cornell.edu
