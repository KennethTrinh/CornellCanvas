You may count a course towards both the ECE major and the CS minor. (If you wish it to count towards the CS minor, you must enroll as CS 4420 and not ECE 4750.)

But within the ECE major, you cannot count one course as two different categories. You must pick one. (Imagine filling out one of the bubbles with "ECE 4750 / CS 4420")

Technically, you should be able to use CS 4420 as an OTE. Whether or not it would count towards following rule (listed on the ECE Program Requirements website) is more of a Prof. Delchamps matter:

One additional option exists for ECE students. A student may, with advisor approval, count one advanced ECE course as an Outside-ECE Technical Elective provided that the course's subject matter lies outside the student's major disciplinary area as determined by the focus of the student's upper-level ECE course work.
