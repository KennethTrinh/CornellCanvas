Author: Aaron Wagner (admin)

Vote Count: 0

It was required as of Fall 2020. The requirement was eliminated in Spring 2021 and replaced with a requirement that you take one additional 3000+ ECE elective. Since 3400 is an 3000+ ECE course, a checklist that satisfies the old requirements satisfies the new ones. So we don't need to even refer to the old requirements anymore, since anyone who follows the handbook from 2020 or before will graduate all the same.
