How can one can tell if a course counts in the Foreign Language (FL) liberal studies category? I assumed such courses would be clearly marked in the catalog, but there are some obvious FL courses that have no liberal studies designation at all (e.g., FREN 1210 and SPAN 1210). So I'm confused. The specific question that is coming up is whether ASL 1101 counts as FL, but the question applies more generally.

This site is kludgy (sp?) but useful: https://apps.engineering.cornell.edu/liberalstudies/FL.cfm Click on FL, and it tells you to look at the "Other Yes" petitioned list, and ASL 1101 appears there as an approved LS course in category FL.
