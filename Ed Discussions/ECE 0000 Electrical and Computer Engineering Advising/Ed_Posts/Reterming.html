E-mail ECE's Undergraduate Coordinator at ugrad-coordinator@ece.cornell.edu indicating that, based on your progress through the program, you think you might be eligible for junior standing. You'll need to submit an ECE Graduation Checklist, as well. ECE will review your progress and, if appropriate, "re-term" you as a junior by notifying the engineering registrar.
