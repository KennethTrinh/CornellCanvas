One more wrinkle: you need a 2.5 GPA across a list of courses including those above --- for the list, see:

https://www.ece.cornell.edu/ece/programs/undergraduate-programs/majors/affiliation

Furthermore, the C+ thing has nuances --- you need a C+ in MATH 2930, PHYS 2213, and at least one of the ECE 2000-level required courses.
