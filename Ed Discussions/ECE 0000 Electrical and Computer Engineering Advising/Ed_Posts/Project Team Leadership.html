ENGRG 4900 seems to be a completely separate seminar course in leadership, not the "team lead" portion that allows a 4th credit as seen in several student project team. If you would like your work completed as part of SYSEN 4900 to count towards the liberal study requirement, I suggest you first talk with the team's faculty advisor and then submit a General Petition to the Engineering Registrar based on the merit of your request.
