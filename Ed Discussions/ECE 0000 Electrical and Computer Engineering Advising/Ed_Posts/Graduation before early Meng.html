"Early M.Eng." means that during one semester you're still finishing undergrad requirements while simultaneously taking courses toward your M.Eng. If you finish undergrad requirements in Fall 2023 and take your first M.Eng. courses in Spring of 2024, you'd be doing "regular M.Eng." starting in Spring 2024 rather than Early M.Eng. starting that semester.
