Please see announcement below for ENGRG 2350 :

Do you feel like you need some structure in your career development?

Consider enrolling in ENGRG 2350 this fall. This 2-credit course is normally only taught in the spring semester so take advantage of this unique fall opportunity!

Topics Include:

  • Identifying your strengths and values

  • Researching employers

  • Developing strong resumes and application materials

  • Effectively communicating your skills and talents

  • Connecting with professionals in your industry of choice

  • Career Decision-making

    • Two sections of this active course are offered- one in-person and one asynchronous- so you can fit it in your busy schedule!

    Please note that asynchronous does NOT mean non-interactive. You will interact with other students and the instructor at times of mutual convenience. The asynchronous course does take time management skills and self-discipline, so it may not be the best fit for students who have struggled with that in the past. For that reason, some students might be better served in the in-person section.
