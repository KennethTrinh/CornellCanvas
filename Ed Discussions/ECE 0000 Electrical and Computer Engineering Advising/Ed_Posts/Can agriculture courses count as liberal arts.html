Here's a link to the currently petitioned and approved liberal-studies courses:

https://apps.engineering.cornell.edu/liberalstudies/ApprovedLiberalStudies.pdf

This page links to lists of petitioned courses, both approved and denied:

https://apps.engineering.cornell.edu/liberalstudies/

The second link also links to all liberal studies courses by LS category --- a separate link for each category. You can search for specific non-petitioned courses there, but you have to search one category at a time.

The college used to post a master list of all liberal studies courses, and they still might, but I've been unable to find it.

A liberal-studies course's catalogue entry usually (but not always) has a LS designation. Like ECON 1110 has SBA-AS, meaning it's an SBA LS course offered by the College of Arts and Sciences.
