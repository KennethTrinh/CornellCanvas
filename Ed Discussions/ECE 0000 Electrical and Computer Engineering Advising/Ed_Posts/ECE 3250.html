Author: Aaron Wagner (admin)

More broadly, the plan is for ECE 3250 is to become a junior-level first course in signals, systems, and related topics. So the course will remain in the catalog, and it will remain a foundation class, but it will no longer have ECE 2200 (which no longer exists) as a prereq. Upper-division courses will continue to list ECE 3250 as a prereq as appropriate.
