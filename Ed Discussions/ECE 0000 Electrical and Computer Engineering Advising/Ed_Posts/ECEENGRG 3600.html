To your first question: it means it can't count either as 3000+ ECE or as OTE (because it's not a technical course). It can count as liberal studies or as AAE.

To your second question: still fine to use as KCM LS. Not CE because it's not primarily a writing class. In fact, I think you need to sign up for ECE 3023 (or something like that) in parallel for it to kill off technical writing.
