Students are only allowed to pursue the Early Admit option in their FINAL SEMESTER of enrollment as an undergraduate student. What semester that may be could vary for some students, as there are those that may end up graduating early. In general, yes, the Early Admit option is mostly for students in what would be their 8th and final semester of enrollment.

For students that are applying for the Early Admit semester to be in the spring, the deadline to submit all completed application materials for consideration would be October 15th.

For students that are applying for the Early Admit semester to be in the fall, the deadline to submit all completed application materials for consideration would be January 15th.

If you have any additional questions, or would like to discuss in detail how the Early Admit option works, please contact me directly at sec36@cornell.edu, and/or come to see me in 223 PH.
