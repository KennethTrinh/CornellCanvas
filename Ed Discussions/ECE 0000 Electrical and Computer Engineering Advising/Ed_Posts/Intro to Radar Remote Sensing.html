Introduction to Radar Remote Sensing still exists as ECE 4870: see courses.cornell.edu (a.k.a. the Courses of Study ).

It is, however, a cross-listed course from EAS. That means the course is owned and operated by EAS (as EAS 4870), but it was deemed (at least at one point in the past) to have enough ECE content for the course to appear as ECE 4870 in our roster whenever EAS chooses to offer it.

EAS was indeed offering EAS 4870 in SP23 until recently, but it seems to have disappeared from the roster. As with all cross-listed courses, we have no control over when (or if) the class is offered. We don't receive warning if it is canceled.

EAS 5870 is still being offered, which is strange because the catalog description says it is supposed to meet simultaneously with EAS 4870/ECE 4870. EAS might have a reason for offering one but not the other, or EAS 4870 might have been deleted by mistake, or perhaps EAS 5870 was supposed to be deleted but wasn't.
