Update: the College said they "... removed the sentence from the website now which should eliminate the confusion." FYI, they discourage students from submitting the form during pre-enroll because (1) they have found that a lot of students change their minds about enrolling in conflicting courses in the interregnum, and (2) once the registrar adds the conflicting courses, it prevents you from making any further changes to your schedule yourself, because your "new" schedule will always be flagged as invalid by the system. So you either need to ask them to make changes for you, or you need to drop one of the courses with the conflict, make the change you want, and then petition to take conflicting courses all over again.
