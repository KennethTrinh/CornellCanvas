CIS isn't "part of engineering," but STSCI 5100 is definitely a 5000-level technical course. An ECE undergrad, for example, could count it as an Outside-ECE Technical Elective. I'm not sure whether the M.Eng. rules require all M.Eng. technical courses to be "in engineering," but I'd imagine not --- that would rule out math and physics courses, for example, and probably some CS. Scott Coldren might have more info.
