Advisor Approved Electives must be from courses 1100 level or higher and cannot double count toward any other degree requirement (so separate from the Liberal Studies requirement). You will want your faculty advisor's permission on what you count as Advisor Approved Electives (6 credits minimum). The College of Engineering only allows optional S grades to be used toward Advisor Approved Electives and Liberal Studies.
