ECE deleted the list when they added ECE 2720, which satisfies the requirement, to the list of required ECE courses. I managed to recover the old list from ECE's Web person --- I've included the text below. Other courses have been approved by petition --- like, I recall that some 3000-level ECON course was approved earlier this year, and there's definitely a MATH course that works. Probability and Statistics Requirement The courses that an ECE major takes to satisfy the ECE Major Requirements or the ENGRD requirement must include at least one course with significant probability content. ECE 2720, ECE 3100, ENGRD/ORIE 2700, CEE 3040, and MSE 5730 are appropriate such courses, but keep in mind that those courses are quite different and are by no means interchangeable. Other courses satisfying the probability and statistics requirement may be allowed by an ECE petition.
