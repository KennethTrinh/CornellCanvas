ECE 3400 was required and now is not required of any student who hasn't taken it yet. When ECE 3400 was required, ECE's upper-level elective requirements called for two 3000+ ECE electives. With ECE 3400 no longer required, ECE's upper-level elective requirements call for three 3000+ ECE electives.
