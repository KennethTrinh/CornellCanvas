You are invited to a townhall for ECE undergraduates, to take place Wed., Aug 31st, from 4:30-5:30 p.m. in 101 Phillips Hall. A very tentative agenda:

  • Introductions: the faces behind the names that you see

  • Welcome remarks from Prof. Alyssa Apsel, Director of ECE

  • State-of-the-major presentation by Prof. Aaron Wagner, Assoc. Director of ECE and Director of Undergraduate Studies:

  • Recent changes to the graduation requirements and why they were made

  • Upcoming expected changes, new course announcements, etc.

  • Tips for navigating life as an ECE: finding research opportunities, graduation checklists, grad school applications, etc.

  • [with occasional polls by show of hands]

  • Blitz introductions from student groups: IEEE, HKN, the Maker club

  • Q&A with Profs. Wagner and Delchamps

    • We will end with refreshments in the hallway outside Phillips 101 and possibly a tour of the Maker spaces for those who are interested.

    You are invited to send questions ahead of time to assoc_director@ece.cornell.edu, especially if your question requires some research to answer. You are also welcome to ask questions in the moment.

    This is a unique opportunity for everyone involved with the ECE undergraduate program to convene face-to-face. I look forward to seeing you all next week.
