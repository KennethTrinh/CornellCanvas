The Learning Strategies Center helps students in courses match with study partners. Here's a quick blurb: Find study partners! Studying with peers is a great way to connect with other Cornell students and is a powerful tool for learning. Finding people to study with can be challenging, and Cornell’s Learning Strategies Center ( LSC) helps match you with study partners. To find out more about study groups and partners, and to sign-up for study partners for class you are in, visit the LSC’s Studying Together webpage.
