Yes, it's possible to petition to potentially have a 5000+ level course offered by another field in engineering added to the Outside ECE Core Course.

Now, that having been said, you will need to make a solid case as to why this should happen, how it will benefit your academic goals and career plans. Petitions are reviewed by the MEng Committee and then they will render a decision.
