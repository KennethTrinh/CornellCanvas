You can set up three equations and 3 unknowns. You know the current through each transistor, and the 60k resistor is identical. That current , I, is one of the unknowns. The current in each transistor can be written in terms of u, C W and L, and Vth, with only Vgs unknown. The Vgs and Vsg are the two other unknowns. Finally, you know the voltage drop . Vgs + Vsg + IR =10V. These give you enough equations to find the three values.
