Hello, You may get confused with the notation. The subscript indicates the type of semiconductor, n- or p-type. Pn: hole concentration in n-type semiconductor [cm^-3] (minority carrier) Pp: hole concentration in p-type semiconductor [cm^-3] (majority carrier) Nn: electron concentration in n-type semiconductor [cm^-3] (majority carrier) Np: electron concentration in p-type semiconductor [cm^-3] (minority carrier) Pn,f > Pn,e means the minority hole concentration in n-type semiconductor in forward bias is larger than that in equilibrium.
