One is given for I_c = 10 mA, and the other was for 50 mA. The saturation voltage is weakly dependent on the base and collector currents. In Lab 2 the traces you took were a max of 5 mA, so you probably saw something slightly greater than what is listed in the datasheets. My data looked closer to 0.4 V. I attributed it to the smaller Ic and Ib compared to what is listed in the datasheet.
