In an individual transistor. W/L is fixed. But in Q1 you are asked to design a new transistor, M_eq, which will behave like the two series connected, and you have total control of W/L in the new transistor. You will need to specify it in terms of the W/L for the M_1 and M )2 transistors. In other wiords, you might say "keep L the same and double W", or "double L and increase W by 72" or whatever you decide, but state your new dimensions in terms of those given W and L.
