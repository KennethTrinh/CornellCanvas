For MOSFETs, you should be able to answer questions like you have seen on the homework 1 and 2, like MOS capacitance questions. You should also be able to identify regions of operations of a given MOSFET configuration. It would not be a bad idea to have an understanding of the semiconductor physics involved with Mosfets, which can help you to take an intuitive approach to the above mentioned questions.
