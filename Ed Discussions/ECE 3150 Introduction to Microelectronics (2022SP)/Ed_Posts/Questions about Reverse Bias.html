1) Under forward bias (i.e. positive voltage applied to p-type region with respect to n-type region), the carriers on either side of the junction will have a greater tendency to move into the depletion region. This results in recombination near the borders of the depletion region and the corresponding carrier region, which reduces the effective width of the depletion region. 2) The negative seems to be in reference to the fact that the figure shows a reverse bias scenario.
