Author: Max McCarthy (staff)

Vote Count: 1

You only need $I_B$ to select the appropriate curve. $I_C$ is the y-coordinate of the point on that curve. If you are referring to the forward active region specifically (approximately horizontal portion), there is a small slope due to the early effect. Again this is just an approximation based on the graph provided, but if your circuit indicates that it is in forward active mode, that should be consistent with your choice of $V_{CE}$. Also be aware that there are other modes of operation, which correspond to different parts of the curve.
