Hi, I would say both expressions are correct. The Boltzmann constant has two versions depending on the units. In the HW, k_b=1.38\cdot10^{-23}\ \left[\frac{J}{K}\right] In the textbook, k_b=8.62\cdot10^{-5}\ \left[\frac{eV}{K}\right] You need to use the one that makes the term inside the exponential unitless.
