I think the question is more asking about the magnitude of the gain rather than the sign, so you may neglect the negative sign - in this case, it doesn’t even matter that much (the speaker will be 180 degrees out of phase with the microphone, but compression and rarefaction of air are interchangeable and won’t affect what you hear)
