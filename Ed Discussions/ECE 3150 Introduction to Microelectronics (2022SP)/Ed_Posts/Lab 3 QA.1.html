Hello, Yes you are supposed to get the transconductance as a function of V_GS, the slope of the tangent line to the I_D vs V_GS graph at each V_GS data point. You may use the following equation to get it. g_m\left(V_{GS}\right)=\frac{I_D\left(at\ the\ next\ V_{GS}\right)-I_D\left(at\ the\ current\ V_{GS}\right)}{\Delta V_{GS}} For the second question, first, get the linear fit of the g_m(V_GS) curve you extracted. The slope of the linear fit function corresponds to k_p. Then from the I_D equation in the inversion region, get the expression of g_m, which is linearly proportional to V_GS. Then you know what k_p is from the slope.
