Hello, one way to approach the problem would be check the cases if the input was largely positive or largely negative and see which diodes are on/off. Once you figure this out, it will be easier to see when (for what input voltage), the diodes change their on/off state. Also, you can use "The method of assumed states". Here, you make an assumption on whether a diode on/off and try to see if that is possible or is contradictory.
