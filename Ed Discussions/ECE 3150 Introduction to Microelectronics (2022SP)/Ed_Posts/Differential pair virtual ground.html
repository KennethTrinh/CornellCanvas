For this layout, are the nodes along the middle still a virtual ground? More generally, will the middle nodes always be virtual grounds even if there is no ideal current bias?

Author: Clifford Pollock (admin)

Yes, so long as the transistors are matched, and the inputs are differential, the central axis of the circuit will always behave like a virtual ground to AC signals. In this case, the currents in M1 and M2 will be identiacal, and equal to half the total current running through M5.
