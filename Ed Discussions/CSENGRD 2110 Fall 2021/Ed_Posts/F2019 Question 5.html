Author: Andrew Chen (student)

If a graph contains K33 or K5 graphs that makes it nonplanar. Here's a picture of what they look like: https://www.nomachetejuggling.com/assets/k33andk5.png .

K5 is a graph with 5 nodes and all of them are connected to each other.

K33 is a bipartite graph with each node from one group of 3 connecting to all three nodes from the other group.
