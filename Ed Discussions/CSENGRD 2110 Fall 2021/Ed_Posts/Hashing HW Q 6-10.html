With chaining, multiple keys may be stored in one bucket (because that bucket contains a list). With open addressing, only one key may go in each bucket; therefore, a key may not end up being stored in its "preferred" bucket. This distinction means that the answers for Qs 6-7 may be different from those to Qs 8-9.
