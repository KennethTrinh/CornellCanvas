Why does C.m(-1) only print "1:" ?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Dave Tirtariyadi (admin)

Vote Count: 0

After printing "1:", you go to the next line. There, you evaluate q / (q+1). But, q+1 evaluates to zero, and you have -1/0, which is an arithmetic error. So, the program shuts down there because you don't have a try-catch surrounding it.
