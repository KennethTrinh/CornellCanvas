You do not need a private field.

Do not copy the linked list into an array. Why do that.

Just reference the linked list itself --in the outer class. Your code should be traversing the linked list, enumerating one value at a time. You will need a field to help you keep track of what has been enumerated and what node to enumerate next. Be sure to put a good comment on that field (the class invariant) to state exactly what has been enumerated thus far.
