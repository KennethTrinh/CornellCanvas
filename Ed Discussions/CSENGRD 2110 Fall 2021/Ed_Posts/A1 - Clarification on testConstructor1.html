You are supposed to end up with 4 test procedures (5, if you make a separate one for assert statements). The first one is for GroupA, the constructor and all the observer methods. Whether you call it testGroupA or testConstructor1 doesn't matter.

Note that testing all the observer methods done as a byproduct of testing the constructor because the only way to get at the field values is through the observer methods.
