You will not have to write down any thrown exception from memory. You should know what the basic ones mean --the names are self-evident. Like these, indicating subclasses with indentation: Error Exception RuntimeException ArithmeticException IndexOutOfBoundsException NumberFormatException ClassCastException IOException
