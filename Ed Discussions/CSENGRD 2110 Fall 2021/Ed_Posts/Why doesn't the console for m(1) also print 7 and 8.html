In order for "7" to be printed, a RuntimeException would have to be thrown from within the try block. In order for "8" to be printed, all exceptions thrown would have to be caught. But presumably you think that "5" should be printed, but not "6". This means that an exception is thrown on the line in between. What try statement encloses that line?
