You should know the most common method names for Java Collection classes ( add() , remove() , size() , get() , poll() , etc.). For more abstract settings you should have a sense for what an "insert" operation would do, for example, but you don't need to memorize specific names; we'll either give you the desired API (with hopefully obvious names), or we'll accept any answer in English that makes it clear what operation you are referring to.
