Which line you put it on doesn't matter. Don't think in terms of lines.

In this case, k is available after the loop.

int k; for (k= 0; k < 1-; k= k+1) {...} after the loop

In this case, k is available NOT after the loop.

for (int k= 0; k < 1-; k= k+1) {...} after the loop
