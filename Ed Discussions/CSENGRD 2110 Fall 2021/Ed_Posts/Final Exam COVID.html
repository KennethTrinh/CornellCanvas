I know you are all being inundated with Ed posts right now, the student body is in crisis. Many of my friends have tested positive this week, let alone within the last 24 hours.

I am requesting the course staff consider a note sheet for everyone on the exam. I do not care whether the exam is in person or online, today or next week, I just want a notes sheet on the exam.

Students right now are in crisis, many of us stressing that we will give COVID to their family and friends and for many that can mean serious consequences. Not a single student right now has their head screwed on right, but there are two hours until the exam still which is enough time for everyone to write down those few lines they would be cramming in their heads in this moment if we were not in a crisis.

Please, please consider helping students out here. I have yet to see any sense of care for mental health here at Cornell. Professors who show kindness in the eleventh hours are the only people who can help.
