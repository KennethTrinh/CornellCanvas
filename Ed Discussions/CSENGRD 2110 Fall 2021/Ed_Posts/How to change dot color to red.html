Author: Curran Muhlberger (admin)

Your logic sounds reasonable here. One way to debug would be to add a println() statement where you change the color to ensure that the desired branch is actually executing. Another thing to be aware of is that the color will only affect subsequent drawing operations (usually assumed to be in that same method). If you fill the circle before changing the color, or previously in another method entirely, then the color change will have no effect (think of it as "switching cans of paint" - it doesn't affect anything you've already painted).
