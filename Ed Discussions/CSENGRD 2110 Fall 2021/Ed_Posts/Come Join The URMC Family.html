The purpose of Underrepresented Minorities in Computing (URMC) is to promote diversity within the computing fields and foster an environment that empowers underrepresented minorities with technological aspirations through career development, community building and academic support. We host many events such as corporate workshops, resume reviews, mock interviews, and many more.

We have a G-Body TOMORROW 9/2 5pm at Gates G01. Come to learn more about what we do and to meet our members. Scan the QR code below to follow us on Instagram and learn more about future events.
