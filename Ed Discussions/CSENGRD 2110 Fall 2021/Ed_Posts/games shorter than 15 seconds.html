This could be indicative of a problem, but it would be helpful to get some more targeted diagnostics, such as: Does your game show 10 different targets? Is each target shown for about the same duration? Do the targets respawn faster and slower when you adjust the speed slider? (as written, these rely on later TODOs, but you could also investigate them with print statements or manually calling functions). If these diagnostics look good, then your program is probably fine; Swing timers are not particularly high-precision, and iirc, they do not preserve phase if an event is late. But if your games are short because you are only showing 9 targets and not 10, that is a problem.
