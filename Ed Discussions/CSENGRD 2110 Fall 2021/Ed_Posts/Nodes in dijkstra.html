Can a node be in both the settled set and far-off set?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Curran Muhlberger (admin)

Vote Count: 0

ENDORSED

The settled, frontier, and far-off sets form a partition of the set of nodes in the graph. No node may be in two different partitions at once.
