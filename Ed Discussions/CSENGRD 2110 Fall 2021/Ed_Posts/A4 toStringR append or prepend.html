They are not contradictory. Do what makes sense: 1. toStringR has to be tested. But it can't be tested until you can create nonempty lists. So, test Prepend and toStringR together, in ONE testing procedure with @Test before it. In A1, you wrote one testing procedure for the first constructor. To get at the fields, it had to use the observer functions. So, as a byproduct of testing the first constructor, you also tested the observer functions. In A4, you write one testing procedure for prepend. Aa a byproduct, it also tests toStringR.
