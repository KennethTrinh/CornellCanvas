Hey everyone!

Interested in sustainability and working on extracurricular projects that have a tangible impact on the world? Apply for CSC! (No resume required, don't be shy :) )

The Cornell Sustainability Consultants (CSC) is an interdisciplinary student group that works with local organizations and industry experts to define not only what it means to be sustainable, but also how to achieve it. CSC serves as a catalyst for sustainability using strategies that range from providing industry-specific recommendations to fostering collaboration both on campus and within local municipalities. We value a connection to our mission and vision with a focus on curiosity, hard-work, and accountability. Students from all majors and years are encouraged to apply. NO EXPERIENCE IS NECESSARY to join our team. We are simply looking for motivated members who are willing to dedicate 4-5 hours per week to their project and team.

If you have any questions about applying or just want to learn more about the opportunity, don't hesitate to reach out at sustainability.cornell@gmail.com. You're also welcome email me personally at emg257@cornell.edu.
