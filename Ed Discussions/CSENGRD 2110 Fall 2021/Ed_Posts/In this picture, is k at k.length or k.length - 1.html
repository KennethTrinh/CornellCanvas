Here, the image depicts b[h..k], which may not be the entire array b.

Since k is an index of array b, there is no notion of k.length.

If the image depicted the entire array b[0..b.length-1] and was drawn as below: h i k b.length _______________________________________________________ b | unknown | s=sum of these elements |

Then k would be at b.length-1, but this is not the case in the image, and you should not assume that h=0 and you should not assume that k = b.length-1.
