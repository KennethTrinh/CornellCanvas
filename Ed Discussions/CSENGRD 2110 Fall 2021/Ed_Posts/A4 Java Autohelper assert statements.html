I am assuming this feedback was for your version of DListTest . What it is saying is that your test was expecting an AssertionError to be thrown under some conditions, but our reference implementation of DList did not throw such an error. Look carefully at the specifications for DList - does it ever specify a situation in which it will throw an AssertionError ? If not, then you should not be testing for such behavior. We handled this a little differently for A1; see #435 for a discussion.
