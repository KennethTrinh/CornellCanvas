You will do better on these it you take the time to draw the objects. Second line: b is class B. Class b implements I2. Therefor, and upward cast, or coercion, from B to I2 will inserted. Third line: This would require a cast from i1 to I2. that is not an upward cast, so it won't be inserted automatically, and this is illegal. Fourth line: Here, the expression (I1) i1 has (static) type I1. So this is similar to the second line; a cast from I1 to I2 will not be inserted.
