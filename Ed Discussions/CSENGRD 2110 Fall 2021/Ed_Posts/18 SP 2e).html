This is a possibility:

Thread 1: load x

Thread 1: x = 2*5 = 10

Thread 1: assign 10 to x

Thread 2: load x

Thread 2: X= 10%3 = 1

Thread 2: assign 1 to x

You can make any combination of the two threads since they are not synchronized.

For instance, you get 10 like this:

Thread 1: load x

Thread 2: load x

Thread 2: X= 5%3 = 2

Thread 1: x = 2*5 = 10

Thread 2: assign 2 to x

Thread 1: assign 10 to x
