Suppose you have a bag of coins, and it contain two: a quarter and a dime. The sum of the bag is 25 + 10: 35. Its product is 25* 10 = 250

Suppose the bag contains only a dime. The sum of the bag is 10. Its product is 10.

Suppose the bag is empty. The sum of the bag is 0. Its product is 1.
