An equivalence relation must always be symmetric, reflexive, and transitive. Any implementation of equals() that does not have these properties is buggy. Examples of such bugs were included on the recent Canvas quiz (you should be able to review your responses). If you follow our template for implementing equals() and compare all fields using either equals() or == as appropriate, then your implementation will most likely satisfy all three of these properties automatically.
