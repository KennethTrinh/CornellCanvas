There is a big difference between radius and radius*2 - a whole factor of 2! The latter of course corresponds to a circle's diameter. You need to choose the right quantity based on the specifications and on the API documentation for the functions involved. The radius of the circle as drawn on the screen must be equal to its radius property in pixels (ignoring the effects of screen magnification).
