The study guide found on the Exams and Canvas Quizzes page of our Canvas website,

https://canvas.cornell.edu/courses/29519/pages/exams-and-canvas-quizzes

says this:

Topics covered on Prelim 2 0. Everything needed for Prelim 1. Read the Pre- lim-1 study guide.

That said, the emphasis is on the topics detailed in the rest of that study guide, with the Prelim 1 stuff being used but not expressly asked. One should, of course, know the definitions and principles. Type study into the JavaHyperText Filter Field, and one entry leads you to this pdf file:

https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/JavaAndDS/files/HowWhatToStudy.pdf

Page 2 contains stuff that by now should be second nature to you.
