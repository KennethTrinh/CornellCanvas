After reading this, please visit JavaHyperText,

https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/JavaAndDS/definitions.html

and type procedure into the Filter Field to see what that word means.

1. No, it is not specific to Java, it just means an method/subroutine that does not return a result but just does something, as opposed to a function, which returns a value.

2. Please don't be concerned with the keyword "static" at this point. It will be explained at the proper time, in a few lectures. There's a lot going on; we can't explain everything immediately.

3. In the same way, don't be concerned about the parameter at this point.
