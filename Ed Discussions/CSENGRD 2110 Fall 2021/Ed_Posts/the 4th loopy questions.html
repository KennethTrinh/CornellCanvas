When does the Lopo terminate? When k = n+1. Substitute n+1 for k. in the invariant z = sum of m..k-1 and you get z = sum of m..n+1-1 which reduces to z = sum of m..n . Remember upon termination, you know !B and invariant. Use that to figure out whether R is true.
