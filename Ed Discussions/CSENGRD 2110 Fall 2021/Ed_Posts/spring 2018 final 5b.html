Is there a reason to use this.name and courseID as opposed to this.name and this.courseID or name and courseID?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: David Gries (admin)

Vote Count: 1

Yes. The field "name" is shadowed by parameter "name" --"name" by itself always refers to the parameter. Hence, to reference the field, "this." is needed.
