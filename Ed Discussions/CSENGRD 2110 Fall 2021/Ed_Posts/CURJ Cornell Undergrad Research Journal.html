Are you involved in undergraduate research? Are you interested in having your work published in a peer-reviewed research journal? The Cornell Undergraduate Research Journal (CURJ) is now accepting submissions for its Fall 2021 issue. CURJ is a bi-annual, peer-reviewed research publication featuring the work of Cornell undergrads. To submit your research paper to be published in CURJ, please fill out our submission form following all submission guidelines at curj.org . The deadline to submit your research paper is 23 December 2021, although we encourage early submissions. Please note: Research done by an undergrad under guidance of a professor, grad student, etc. is eligible for publication with proper permissions. Email curj@cornell.edu with any questions, and feel free to visit our instagram at @curj_cornell and join our mailing list at tinyurl.com/curjmail to receive updates.
