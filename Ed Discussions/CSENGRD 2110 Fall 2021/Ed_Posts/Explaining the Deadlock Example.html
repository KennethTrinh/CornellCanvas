It is not that complicated. Edsger and Tony will both reach out for fork 0. One of them will get it, and the other will simply wait. If Edsger gets it and David gets fork 3, then there will be a computer scientist between Edsger and David who gets to finish their dinner. If Edsger gets it and David fails to acquire fork 3, David waits and Edsger picks up fork 4 and eat. If Tony gets it, Edsger waits and it will depend on whether David acquires fork 3 and so on recursively.
