Author: David Gries (admin)

NO, it is not circular.

For a node f in the frontier set, d[f] is the length of the shortest path over paths that consist of a bunch of red (settled) nodes followed by ONE blue node:

(v=s1, s2, s3, ..., sn, f)

So, d[f] may not be the length of the shortest path over ALL paths from v to f.

BUT, if d[f0] is the smallest d[..] value for nodes in the Frontier set, the theorem says that d[f0] is the shortest path length over ALL paths to f0.
