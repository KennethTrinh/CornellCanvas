Good question. You are doing what a programmer should do when presented with an issue about a class they are writing for a client. A full-blown class to be used by other programs would have many more preconditions than we give in A1, such as, as you say, restrictions on the birth date. However, we are not creating such a complete, finished product but only a class that introduces you to the basics. A1 has what we consider the minimum features to get you to learn about OO programming. Nothing more should be added. Therefore, please use ONLY the specifications of the methods as given, and therefore assert statements only for the Preconditions given in the specifications. Don't be concerned with other things, such as restrictions on birthdates.
