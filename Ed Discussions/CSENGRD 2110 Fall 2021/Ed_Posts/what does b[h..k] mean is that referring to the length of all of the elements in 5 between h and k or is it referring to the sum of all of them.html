You had slides that said something like this

4..6 is the set of values 4, 5, and 6 4..5 is the set of values 4 and 5 4..4 is the set of one value: 4 4..3 is the set of 0 values.

Therefore, b[h..k] is the array segment consisting of b[h], b[h+1], b[h+2], ..., b[k].

And, something like. b[h..k] <= 0 means: Every element of array segment b[h..k] is less than or equal to 0.
