On Macs, the native look-and-feel is used by default, rather than the cross-platform "Ocean/Metal" look-and-feel that is the default on Linux and Windows. This is okay - as long as you see a "save file" dialog, it meets the specifications. It doesn't need to look pixel-for-pixel identical, since operating systems are all a bit different.
