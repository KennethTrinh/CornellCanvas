The exact text of the error, and possibly even the subclass of exception, will depend on your operating system. Therefore, "Access is denied" is fine if that's what's in the exception's message on your computer.

As for including the exception class in the dialog text (and not just the title), that's an optional embellishment. Strictly speaking, the handout only requires the exception's message and not its type.
