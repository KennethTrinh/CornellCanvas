You are being haphazard about your wording.

A test case is one call of a method, say, a test of its result.

Therefore, in testing toStringR and prepend together in one testing procedure you will have several test cases.

The testing of the constructor is already given to you, right?

Test toStringR and prepend together, in one testing procedure, with @Test before it.

Then, write a separate testing procedure for each of the other methods you have to implement.
