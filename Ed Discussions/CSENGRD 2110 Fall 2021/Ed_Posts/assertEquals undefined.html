I had the same problem too and if you want to keep the original A2Test.java try right-clicking A2Test.java and go to Build Path>Configure Build Path... then click "Libraries" in the tabs above(Source, Projects, Libraries, Order and Export...). Check if you have JUnit 5 under Classpath. If you don't, click "add library" and add JUnit 5.
