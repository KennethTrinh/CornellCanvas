If I understand you correctly, you are currently moving your files out of your Eclipse workspace and onto your desktop. Here are some alternatives:

  • Upload the file directly from your Eclipse workspace directory. There should be no need for the file to live on your Desktop to be able to upload it.

  • If you really want it on your Desktop, you can copy-paste the file there using keyboard shortcuts, right-click options, or menu options.

  • There may also be a modifier key you can hold while clicking-and-dragging to make a copy. On Windows, you can hold "Control" when dragging to do this, or you can right-click drag to get a menu of options, including "move" and "copy". Classmates, feel free to chime in if you know the equivalent options on a Mac.

  • If you're comfortable with Terminal commands, you can use cp instead of mv .

    • I recommend the first option, but it's good to be comfortable with a range of different ways of interacting with your computer.
