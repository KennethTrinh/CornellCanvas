I assume that the specification says "Node u is not visited". If that precondition is not there, I would answer differently. This has nothing to do with efficiency. If you did not have that statement, if you do not start out by visiting u, when WOULD you visit u? At the end is illogical. I don' know what else to day. Second, the statement sequence visit node v; dfs v; is absolutely wrong, because recursive call violates the precondition.
