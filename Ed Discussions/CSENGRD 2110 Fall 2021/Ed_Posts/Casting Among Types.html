In this expression, the - is the unary negation operator, not subtraction. This example shows how unary operators (which include both casts and negation) are parsed, namely, right-to-left. So the order of operations is:

  • 4 + 5 → 9 (int)

  • 9 → 9.0f (float)

  • 9.0f → -9.0f (float)

  • -9.0f → -9 (int)

    • ------------------------------------
