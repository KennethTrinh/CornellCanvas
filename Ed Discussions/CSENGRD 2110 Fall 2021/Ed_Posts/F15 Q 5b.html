We don't create another array because that is not needed. Suppose the heap is in b[0..i]. The typical way one polls is to 1.Swap b[0] and b[i]. That puts the largest value in the heap in b[i] 2. i= i-1. That take b[i] out of the heap, and it is in its final position. 3. Bubble b[0] down in b[0..i]
