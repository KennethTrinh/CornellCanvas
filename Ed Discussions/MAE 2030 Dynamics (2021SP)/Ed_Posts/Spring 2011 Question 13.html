Author: Dong Junyi (staff)

Vote Count: 1

x_D double dot equals x_A double dot because x_D - x_A = constant (1) since the two points A and D are rigidly connected. Then, if you take derivative on both sides of (1), you get: x_D dot - x_A dot =0 and x_D double dot - x_A double dot =0.
