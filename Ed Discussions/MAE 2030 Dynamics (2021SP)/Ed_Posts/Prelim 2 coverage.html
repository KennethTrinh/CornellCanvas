For this course, all prelims and the final exam are inclusive, possibly covering any material from this course, or its prerequisites, up through any homework handed in by the time of the test and any lectures given by the time of the test. These tests are closed book, closed notes, no internet, no student-made "cheat sheet". Bring to the test food and writing materials. If you are taking online, you need a zoom camera at a distance of about 5 feet from you that shows you, your work area, and the front of your screen. Log on to the course recitation zoom link (from Canvas). Your work area needs to be clean and simple looking. At the end of the test, from home or in person, you will make a single multi-page pdf scan of your test. Not jpg. Not single pages. Practice this ahead of time. If you are doing this from home, switch your zoom camera to being your computer camera showing you scanning your exam.
