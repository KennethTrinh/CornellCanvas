Alison, I'll write more later, but first read my little essay about grading, if you have not already. The main issues with your idea are:

  • changing the rules midcourse (which I would do if I thought it was a good idea, and all students agreed);

  • How much grades are supposed to be about effort and how much about your knowledge.

    • At present, my main view about giving homework grades at all is that homework grades are a motivator. Mostly though, you should do the HW to learn. And you should do it well to learn better. And if you know a better way to learn, you can still get a good grade (up to A+) in this course without doing any homework.
