Hi folks,

You should have gotten an email sometime recently from the College of Engineering to provide feedback on the 2030 TAs. You can access this survey at http://www.engineering.cornell.edu/TAEval/survey.cfm until March 29th (the Monday after the next) at 5:00 PM.

As a quick tip, positive feedback is most helpful when it makes one of us say "Oh, I should keep doing this particular thing that the students liked", and negative feedback is most helpful when it's constructive, i.e. when it points out something where we can say "Hmm, I/We should be doing this particular thing better". General positive feedback also makes us TAs very happy, so feel free to show us some love if you feel generally happy/satisfied with our work. (I am, of course, biased).

Thanks in advance for your feedback, everyone!
