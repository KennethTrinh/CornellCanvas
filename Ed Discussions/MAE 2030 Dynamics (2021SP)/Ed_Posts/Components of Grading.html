Hi:

We will follow the intent of that grading scheme if the numbers are not quite right. Nobody has to do any projects to get a drop or two.

As for projects, people who do projects just to raise their grade are wasting their time. The projects are to reward people who want credit for pursuing their interests. People just trying to raise their grades are better off using their time to better learn the material for the final exam.
