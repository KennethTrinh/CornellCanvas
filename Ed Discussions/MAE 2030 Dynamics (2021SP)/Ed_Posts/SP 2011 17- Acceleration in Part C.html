Without following the details of your comment, there is this: All of this sort of problem can be done multiple ways using one or another moving frame or using polar coordinates or using relative or absolute angles. In each approach you get the same final answer, but the individual terms are different. Check your answer by two different methods. If they disagree, you have a mistake in at least one of the methods.
