In Q3b, we will have two chances to enqueue E, once when we expand C, once when we expand F, which are all supposed to happen before we expand E. We're supposed to enqueue E when we expand C at the first place, and in this case E's predecessor will be marked as C, but when we expand F, do we still enqueue E and mark/update its predecessor to F? This will make a difference in the final path where we try to find the predecessor for each node along the path.
