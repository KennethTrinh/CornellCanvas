If $x_i$ is true $\neg x_i$ shuld be false and vice-versa. The connections to the $N$ node assure neither $x_i$ nor $\neg x_i$ are the neutral color, i.e. they are each true or false. The connection between $x_i$ and $\neg x_i$ ensures if one is true the other is false.
