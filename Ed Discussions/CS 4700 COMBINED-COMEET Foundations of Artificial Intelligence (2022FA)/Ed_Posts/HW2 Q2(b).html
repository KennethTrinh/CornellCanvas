No "proof" is necessary for this problem, just reasoning about the nature of the longest path problem. The goal is to identify what properties of the longest path problem invalidate fixes to uniform cost search as a solution method. Solving shortest path vs. longest path problems (and what methods might be useful/efficient for each) and, importantly, whether those problems can be solved in polynomial time or not was discussed in the "problem solving by search" lecture in class. I would recommend returning to the lecture slides to gain an understanding for why an efficient algorithm might not work in this case.
