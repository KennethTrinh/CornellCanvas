In question 2 of (2), we want clauses to satisfy: each team plays at most once per week. For simplicity, let's fix, team ($i$) and week ($k$). So, the clauses would be as follows:

\begin{align*}&((\neg X_{i1k} \lor \neg X_{i2k}) \land (\neg X_{i1k} \lor \neg X_{i3k}) \land \dots \land (\neg X_{i1k} \lor \neg X_{iNk}))\\&\land ((\neg X_{i2k} \lor \neg X_{i3k}) \land (\neg X_{i2k} \lor \neg X_{i4k}) \land \dots \land (\neg X_{i2k} \lor \neg X_{iNk})) \\&\text{~}\vdots\\&\land((\neg X_{i(N-1)k} \lor \neg X_{iNk}))\end{align*}
