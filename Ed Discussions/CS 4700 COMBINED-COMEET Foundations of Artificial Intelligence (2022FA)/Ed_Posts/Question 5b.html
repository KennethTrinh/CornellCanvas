I'm not sure I fully understand your question. In 5b, You aren't trying to show any elements are equal. You want to give a set of relations such that the domain with elements $D = \{a,b,c\}$ satisfies the axioms. For example, $S(a,c), S(b,c), S(c,a)$ is the right format for an answer but is not the right answer as $S(a,c)$ and $S(b,c)$ violate the axiom $\forall x \forall y \forall z ( S(x,z) \land S(y,z) ) \Rightarrow (x = y)$.
