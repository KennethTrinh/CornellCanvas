Hi everyone, We just published grades for the first assignment on Gradescope and added solutions under the Homework module on Canvas . Overall, people did very well with a mean of 94%. Regrade requests are open until 11:59pm on Tuesday, October 11th.

The most common issue was not fully answering a question, ex. not providing an example or justification when they were asked for. There also seemed to be some confusion on the reinforcement learning question. The most common errors were:

  • Stating that playing against a stronger opponent would be equal or better to playing against yourself because you can learn from the stronger player's moves

  • Stating that learning would be slower without mentioning that it can become prohibitively expensive, so no learning would occur for complex games like chess
