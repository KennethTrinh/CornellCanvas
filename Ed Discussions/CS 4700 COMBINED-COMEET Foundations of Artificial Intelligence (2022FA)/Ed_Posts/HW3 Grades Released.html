Most issues were with the SAT encoding question where the most common issue was only showing one direction for a bidirectional implication, ex. for 2a only showing one of 'at most one team plays in each subslot' and 'at least one team plays in each subslot'. Solutions are posted under the Modules tab and regrade requests are open until Friday, November 11th at 11:59pm .
