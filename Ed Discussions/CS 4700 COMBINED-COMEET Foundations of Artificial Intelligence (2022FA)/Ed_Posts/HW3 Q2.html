Nothing profound. Each slot has two sub slots. Those are also numbered. So, you have subslot #1 and subslot #2 that form Slot #1. Then subslot #3 and subslot #4, that form Slot #2. Etc So, if a team plays in odd numbered slot L. Then its opponent is in subslot (L +1) (even). -- Bart
