Hi,

Perhaps you've not yet discovered that the absorption cross section of water is given in Table II.3 in the back of the book. Notice that this problem asks for the neutron absorption rate.

You would expect to be able to calculate the cross sections of the H2O molecule by adding the cross sections of the constituents. However, you cannot do that for the scattering cross section for this particular molecule (and a few other important ones). Please see example 3.6 at the end of Section 3.3. That's why it is in the table - it must be obtained experimentally.
