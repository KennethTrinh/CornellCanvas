This is a consequence of the complementary slackness condition. In the primal SVM problem, you have the constraint $y_i(w^Tx_i + b) \geq 1$. The complementary slackness condition for this tells us that $\alpha_i(1 - y_i(w^Tx_i + b) ) = 0$. This forces $\alpha_i=0$ for all points that are not on the margin line.
