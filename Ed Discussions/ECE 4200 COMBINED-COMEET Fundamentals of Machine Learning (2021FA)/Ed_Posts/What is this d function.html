d is the distance of the point X from the hyperplane (w,t). We have d\left(X,\ \overline{w},\ t\right)=\ \frac{\left\{X\cdot\overline{w}-t\right\}}{\ \left|\ \overline{w}\right|} and now for us we have taken the normalization to make the denominator = 1.
