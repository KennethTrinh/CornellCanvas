1e: True (this in in learning theory, which we did not cover much). The idea is that if you have infinite samples, you know the distribution completely and that is what you train for.

8: learning theory, ignore

9: There are many ways to do this.

a. Sum of positive semidefinite matrices is positive semidefinite. Use this to obtain the result.

b. Let Phi_1 be the maping corresponding to K1, and Phi_2 be the mapping corresponding to K2. Then if you concatenate the two mappings to get Phi = [ Phi_1 Phi_2], then the kernel function you get is K1+K2.
