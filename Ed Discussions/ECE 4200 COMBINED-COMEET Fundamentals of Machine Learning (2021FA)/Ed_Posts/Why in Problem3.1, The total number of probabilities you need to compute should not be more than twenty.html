You are correct and the question is also correct. P(sunny|yes)+P(rainy|yes)+P(overcast|yes)=1, ... P(strong|no)+P(weak|no)=1 Now with these dependencies you should compute one fewer number per attribute (4) per label (2) which gives 8 fewer computations. Computing 12 things sounds easier than computing 20, so we put this.
