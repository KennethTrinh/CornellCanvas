In practice usually, you would feed in a "batch" of samples, and then update the weights.

Say, you are having 1000 samples and decide to run the training for 100 epochs, and feed the data with batch size 100.

Then you are updating the model 1000/100=10 times per epoch, and a total of 100*10=1000 updates would happen in your training.

Of course, you can define the batch size to be 1, then this is what you described in the beginning.
