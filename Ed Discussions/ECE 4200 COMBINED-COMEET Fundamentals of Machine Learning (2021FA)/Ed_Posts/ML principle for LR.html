Great question. ML: you find the model that maximizes the data probability and no consideration for prior. In Logistic regression, your model is discriminative, ir you model p(y|x) and from data you find the best model that fits teh data. MAP: the key thing here is having a prior, and you update this prior distribution upon observing data. Hope this helps.
