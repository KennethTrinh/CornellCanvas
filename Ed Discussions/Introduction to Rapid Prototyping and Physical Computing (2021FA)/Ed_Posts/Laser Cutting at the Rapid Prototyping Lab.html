Title: Laser Cutting at the Rapid Prototyping Lab

Author: Felix Shi (staff)

Date: 2021-12-08T08:51:59.178647+11:00

Category: General

Vote Count: 2

There is a laser cutter in the Rapid Prototyping Lab in the basement of Upson. Email them (cornellrpl@gmail.com) a DXF of the sketch you want to cut out. Go during open hours (see the schedule at https://cornellrpl.wixsite.com/cornellrpl ) and bring your material. You will be moved to the top of the queue and the lab technicians will cut it out for your immediately. Hope this helps!
