Hello, I have received many questions about the registration process for this course. As in the past, students will be registered at once after having settled on a group, a project topic, and a weekly meeting time with one of our TAs. This process should take place in the second week of the semester. We can accommodate 40 students in the Fall, and I give priority to students: who need the class to graduate this semester; for whom this is the last time they can take the class; two applied in the past but did not get in; To simplify the management of this information, please complete this form as soon as possible. In the meantime, you should attend a class and monitors Ed and Canvas for announcements. Looking forward to seeing you all on Monday the 30th of August. Francois.
