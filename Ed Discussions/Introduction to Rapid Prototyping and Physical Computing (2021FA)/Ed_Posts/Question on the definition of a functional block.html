Are electronics and software considered their own functional blocks, or are they considered part of the hardware block that they control? For instance, if one of our blocks is a valve system, would the electronics and software needed to control the valves be combined in that block, or would it (and the other electronics/software in the system) be considered a separate block?

I will consider them as different blocks for this class. In practice you will have the following units:

  • Hardware components;

  • Electronic controls for the hardware components are often aggregated on one circuit board (or breadboard)

  • The MCU/Software running on the show

    • This will reflect how you are going to build the final system.
