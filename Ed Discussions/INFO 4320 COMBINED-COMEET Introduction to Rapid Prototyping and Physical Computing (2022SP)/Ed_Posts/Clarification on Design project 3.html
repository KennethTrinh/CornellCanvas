I got a question on whether you can import and use online gear design in your 3D design project. The answer is yes. You can use and import the online design of gear in your project. However, the imported item will not count as one of the 6 parts which are required for this project. In other words, besides the imported gears, you need another 6 parts of your design to satisfy this requirement. Please let me know if you have any further questions.
