Any project you can draw ideas from (pros/cons/etc.) that have previously worked to solve a problem similar to the one you are trying to solve, or accomplish the task you are trying to accomplish in your design. You are not going to follow a tutorial on how to build what you are trying to build, but take inspiration from these previous implementations. Your project should be entirely your own.
