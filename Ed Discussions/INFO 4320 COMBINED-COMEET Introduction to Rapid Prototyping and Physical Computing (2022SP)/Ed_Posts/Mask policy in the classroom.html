Please wear a face mask in class, which is the university policy. Thank you. Beginning Monday, March 14, face mask requirements will be lifted for the majority of on-campus locations. High-quality masks will, however, continue to be required in the following spaces and circumstances: Students and instructors in classrooms, laboratories and similar teaching settings. Faculty who are able to maintain physical distance from others while teaching may choose to do so unmasked. https://covid.cornell.edu/updates/20220311-mask-restrictions.cfm
