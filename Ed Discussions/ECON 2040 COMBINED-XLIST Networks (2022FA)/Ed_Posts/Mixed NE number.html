Player A's probabilities for strategies X and Y are : P(X) = p and P(Y) = 1-p

And Player B's probabilities for strategies X* and Y* : P(X*) = q and P(Y*) = 1-q

Is this considered 1 mixed Nash equilibrium or 2 mixed Nash equilibriums?
