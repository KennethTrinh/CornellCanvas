For a strategy to be dominant, do all of its payoffs have to be the largest one or can it just be mostly dominant with the exception of one strategy for instance?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Mary Kolbas (admin)

Vote Count: 1

Player 1's dominant strategy has to be the best strategy against all of Player 2's options, being either the largest payoff or equal to any of Player 1's other options. ( Strictly dominant strategies mean strictly largest, not allowing equal/indifferent comparisons.)
