If the bidders have the same bids, for example, 10 and 10, the winner will be chosen randomly. If the reserve price is 5, does the winner pay 5 or 10?

The winner pays the maximum of the second highest price and the reserve price. Like you mention, suppose the reserve price is 5. If there are two bids (10, 10), then the second highest price is 10 (joint first). Thus, the winner will pay 10.
