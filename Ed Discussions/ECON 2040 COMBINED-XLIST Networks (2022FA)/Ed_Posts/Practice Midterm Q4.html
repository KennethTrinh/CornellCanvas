Can a price be increased such that a buyers valuation for a product is less than zero?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Cameron Kull (admin)

Vote Count: 1

A price can be increased so that a buyer's value for that item is less than zero, but that buyer would not be the one to purchase that item because it would put them at a loss for making the purchase.
