The description of HW5 1b gives a bit of setup of how to deal with one item and multiple buyers. You can create "fictional" items of value 0 that are assigned to the remaining buyers who didn't get the one item. Then, calculate harm as you would normally do so in the VCG procedure. (You will find that some components are 0, since several buyers are getting a "fictional" item of value 0.)
