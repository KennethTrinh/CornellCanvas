You can draw more edges - the edges in the Figure are simply showing you roads, but the enemy/ally relationship edges are another whole graph. Since +/- relationships can exist between "geographically far" nodes on the original Figure, make sure you consider all triangles, including those that aren't next to each other. #112 may also be relevant to your question.
