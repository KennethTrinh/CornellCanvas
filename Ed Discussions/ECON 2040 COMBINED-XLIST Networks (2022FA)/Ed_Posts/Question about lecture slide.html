Your statement is true, though I'm not sure it's obvious why (you may want to explain it on an exam). If (A,B) or (B,A) is a NE, then that means the best response of a player to the other player's strategy is to choose the other strategy. This means that in an evolutionary state, where most of the people play one strategy, the best response is to pick the other, making the initial strategy not evolutionarily stable.
