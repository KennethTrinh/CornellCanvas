In problems like these, I don't believe we'd ever have "some" get traded; either all or none of a type of good get purchased. We assume that we have enough buyers for the goods, so that we really only need to compare expected values (whether a buyer would wish to buy or a seller would wish to sell knowing their expected value)

As an aside, in the market, the proportions of types of goods is known by all parties
