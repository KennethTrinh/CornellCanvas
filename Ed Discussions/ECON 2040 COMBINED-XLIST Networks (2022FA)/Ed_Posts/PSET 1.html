If a node does not have two strong ties so it does not violate the STC property, does this mean the node satisfies the property anyways even if it has one strong tie and one weak tie?

Author: Mary Kolbas (admin)

You're correct - a node with one strong tie and one weak tie does not violate the STC Property. Since this node doesn't have two strong ties, it vacuously satisfies STCP.
