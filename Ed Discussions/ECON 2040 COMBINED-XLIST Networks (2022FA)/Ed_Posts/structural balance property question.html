By the definition in Chapter 5.1, the structural balance property does not apply for graphs of 1 or 2 nodes. However, it does apply for graphs that have 3 or more nodes. Here's the definition: "Structural Balance Property: For every set of three nodes, if we consider the three edges connecting them, either all three of these edges are labeled +, or exactly one of them is labeled +."
