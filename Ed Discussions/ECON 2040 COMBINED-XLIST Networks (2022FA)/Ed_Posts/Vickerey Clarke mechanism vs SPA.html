To clarify, there are three methods that we've talked about:

Single-Unit Auction

  • Second Price Auction

    • Multi-Unit Auction

  • VCG

  • Generalized Second Price Auction

    • Both VCG and the Generalized Second Price Auction will yield the same matching in the case of a Single Unit (making them "like" SPA). However, VCG will charge different prices. The reason we use VCG instead of the Generalized Second Price Auction when transitioning to multiple units is that it successfully ensures that truthful bidding is a dominant strategy, whereas GSPA does not
