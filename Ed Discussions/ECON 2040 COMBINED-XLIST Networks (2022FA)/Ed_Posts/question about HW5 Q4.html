For Q4(a) What is the definition of Nash Equilibrium? Does Market Clearing price mean Nash Equilibrium?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Aidan McNay (student)

Vote Count: 0

A Nash Equilibrium means that no buyer would benefit from bidding a different value. This does not follow from Market Clearing Prices - in fact, for Question 4, all of these prices are market-clearing (each buyer gets an item), but you can guess from the question that not all of them will be Nash Equilibria. A market can be cleared with a buyer being able to do better by taking a different action

Author: Aidan McNay (student)

Vote Count: 0

Correct - a matching (along with a set of prices) is not a Nash Equilibria if ANY buyer can do better by changing their bid. It is only a NE when none of the buyers have any incentive to switch their bid (they are all bidding a best response to the others' bids)
