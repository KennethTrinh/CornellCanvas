To demonstrate this, let's just consider the price pair of (0,4). Looking at how the payoffs are adjusted (decreasing the payoff for seller B by 4), we get:

\begin{matrix}\text{Seller}&\text{Buyer}&\text{Payoff for Seller A}&\text{Payoff for Seller B}\\a&v&7&6\\b&w&8&5\end{matrix}

Note how both buyers now prefer Seller A! This can be repeated for the others, but a useful thought for the question is considering why they would be the same, without going through the table?
