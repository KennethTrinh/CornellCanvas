Why (y,y) is a Nash equilibrium? Thanks.

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Varun Gande (student)

If player A plays Y, B's best response is to choose Y (or X). If player B plays Y, player A's best response is to choose Y (or X). Since Y is a best response to the other player's Y (for both players), (Y,Y) is a Nash equilibrium.
