Recall how we went about this in lecture, where the person's decision depended on which marble they drew. There was a point reached when a person's best guess didn't depend on what they drew. This would mean that they are no longer "influenced by their signal", where being "influenced" means that your best decision depends on the signal you receive (for clarification, look to the definition - we get signals as our private information and the choices (decisions) of those before us)
