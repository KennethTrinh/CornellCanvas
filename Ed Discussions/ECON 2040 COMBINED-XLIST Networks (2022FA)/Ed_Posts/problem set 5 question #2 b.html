One thing I would add is that you can think of VCG price as the harm that a bidder does by taking a slot. The advertisers' values are still there, but the search engine doesn't know. The way how VCG achieves price distribution is to use this procedure and set the price that can be interpreted as the harm of a specific bidder taking a slot. I hope this helps you understand the question better.
