In the GSP, if an advertiser bids the ith highest bid, he will get the ith best slot and will have to pay the (i+1)th highest bid. So if advertiser a bids 5 (his true value), then he gets slot a and has to pay 3. So his net value is 6*(5-3) = 12, because he values clicks at 5-3 and the clickthrough rate for slot a is 6. It turns out there's a better alternative for a that isn't bidding their true value. What would happen if a bids 2?
