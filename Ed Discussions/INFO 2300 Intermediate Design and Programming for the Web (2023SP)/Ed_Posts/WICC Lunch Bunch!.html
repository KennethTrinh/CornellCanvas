Are you an underclassman student interested in computer or information science? Want to get to know professors across a broad range of disciplines including machine learning, theory, operations research, information science and more? Hoping to make friends who are also passionate about CIS? Want free lunch? Join the WICC Lunch Bunch Program! Geared especially toward underclassmen women and minorities, this program provides a fantastic opportunity to meet with a small group of peers once a week for a conversation with a Cornell professor. Participants will also be considered active WICC members, opening them up to new perks and opportunities. Lunch Bunch will run from the week of February 13th to April 28th, tentatively. Apply at https://forms.gle/ZFVzNueQMfNBCRoZ6 by February 6th ! Feel free to reach out to Shreya Ponugoti(sp843) and Jackie Chin(jc2745) if you have any questions!
