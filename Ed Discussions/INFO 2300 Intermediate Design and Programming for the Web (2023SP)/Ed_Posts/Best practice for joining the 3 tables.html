For Project 3, you shouldn't need to JOIN three tables. If you are, then 1) you might be doing more than is required for the project, or 2) your schema may be structured suboptimally. I would suggest visiting office hours for clarity.

However, if you really need to JOIN three tables, then you simply add two JOIN clauses to the query. That's really all there is to it.
