The context is important here. The quote you pulled here is from a section labeled " Include seed data in your database." This is saying that your seed entries need at least two tags. I have clarified the requirement. It's true that the insert form needs to support tagging an entry with all available tags. I understand that it may not make sense for your design, however the learning objective of a one to many relationship is what's important here. Please see the "Each catalog entry should be able to be tagged by every tag in the database" requirement.
