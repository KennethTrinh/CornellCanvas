A favicon image is NOT required for the project. You are welcome to include a favicon in order to give your website a polished appearance. One way of doing this is by adding the <link> element to your index.html file right below the <title> element. 
 <link rel="icon" type="image/png" href="PATH_TO_FAVICON.png">
