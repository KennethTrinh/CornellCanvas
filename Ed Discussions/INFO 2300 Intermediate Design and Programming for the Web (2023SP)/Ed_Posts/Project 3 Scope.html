This is technically okay. However, this can make grading difficult and sometimes causes grading issues/mistakes. You've got two options: 1. Leave out the extra features for the project, and then add them after you submit your project. No concerns with the approach. 2. Add in the advanced features now and communicate in the design journey what's not implemented. Make it very clear in the design journey what you want us to grade and what should not be graded. Understand that mistakes may happen, and that you may need to submit a regrade request.
