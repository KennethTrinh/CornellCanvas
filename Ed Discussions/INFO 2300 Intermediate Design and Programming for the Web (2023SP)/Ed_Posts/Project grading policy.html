Your Project 3 grade is p3m1 + p3m2 + p3m3 + p3fin. The milestones are graded for completion and the final submission is graded for correctness. So your complete Project 3 grade is 30 points + 100 points = 130 points total. You can calculate out what you think is a low milestone grade, and how it will affect your overall project grade.
