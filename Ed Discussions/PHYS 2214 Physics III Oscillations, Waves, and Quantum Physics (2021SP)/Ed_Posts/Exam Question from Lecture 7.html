Generally, standing waves on strings need to have two fixed ends, and therefore symmetric boundary conditions. When we talked about reflection from the end of a string, we did imagine that it might be possible to rig up a free boundary condition with something like a frictionless ring on a pole that can move up and down while maintaining tension in the string, but this is not really something that is possible to implement in practice.
