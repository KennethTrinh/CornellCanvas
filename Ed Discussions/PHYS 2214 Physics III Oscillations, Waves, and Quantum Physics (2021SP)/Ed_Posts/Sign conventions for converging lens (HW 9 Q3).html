The Unit 19 screen shot does seem to have a typo. "the image will be real (d_o>0)" is true because the object distance is positive. More to the point, however, is that the image distance is positive: (d_i>0). When the image is on the same side of the lens as the object, the image distance is negative--for both converging and diverging lenses. When the image is on the opposite (far) side of the lens from the object, the image distance is positive--this only occurs with converging lenses.
