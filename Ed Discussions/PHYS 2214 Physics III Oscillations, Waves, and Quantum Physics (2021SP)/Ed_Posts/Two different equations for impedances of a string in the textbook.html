Each of those equations for Z are equivalent. Knowing that for a string,

v\ =\ \sqrt{\frac{\tau}{\mu}}

we can substitute this into the bottom equation to get

Z=\frac{\tau}{v}=\mu v\ =\mu\sqrt{\frac{\tau}{\mu}}=\sqrt{\mu\tau}
