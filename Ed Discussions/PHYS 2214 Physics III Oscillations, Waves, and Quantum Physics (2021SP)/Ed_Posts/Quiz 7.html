Author: Dan Ralph (admin)

Vote Count: 0

Since 3 of the phasors point in the positive direction along the real axis and 2 point in the negative direction, the total sum of the five phasors is just 1 $E_0$, compared to 5 $E_0$ at the principal maximum where all of the signals are in phase. Since the intensity goes like the square of the total electric field, the difference in intensity is a factor of 1/25.
