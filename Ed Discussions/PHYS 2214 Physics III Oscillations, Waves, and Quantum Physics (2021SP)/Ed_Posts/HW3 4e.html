Yes, for this example of an ideal string the wave speeds are the same. The moving 'nodes' [true nodes would remain in place] travel along with the long wavelength. These questions foreshadow what we will see in a couple weeks when studying non-ideal media. In non-ideal media the so-called 'phase velocity' wave speed (applicable to the short wavelength) differs from the so-called 'group velocity' wave speed (applicable to the long wavelength).
