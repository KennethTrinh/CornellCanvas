Good catch. The correct answer is the one that includes the factor of 1/2. You can check this by considering the case where the force and the velocity are in-phase, so that the power averaged over one period is

P_{av}=\frac{1}{T_D}\int_0^{T_D}F_0\cos\left(\omega t\right)v_0\cos\left(\omega t\right)dt=\frac{1}{2}F_0v_0

because the average value of (cosine squared) is 1/2.
