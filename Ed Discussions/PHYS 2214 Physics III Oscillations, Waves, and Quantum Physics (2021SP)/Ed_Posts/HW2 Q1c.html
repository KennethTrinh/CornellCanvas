Author: Dan Ralph (admin)

Vote Count: 1

No. If you try this you will see that you don't get the same answer. The procedure for finding the frequency that gives a maximum is to take a derivative with respect to frequency and determine a value or values for which the derivative is zero. If you try substituting in values before you take the derivative, that is not mathematically equivalent.
