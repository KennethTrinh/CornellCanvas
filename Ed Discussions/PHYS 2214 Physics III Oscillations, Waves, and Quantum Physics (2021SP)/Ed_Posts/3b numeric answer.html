My guess (based on one-on-one office hour exchanges) is that some students are using a crazy form of calculus. Starting from $E=hc/\lambda$, they arrive at $\Delta E \;``="\; hc/\Delta \lambda$. The correct formula, of course, is $$\Delta E=\left(\frac{d}{d\lambda}(hc/\lambda)\right)\Delta \lambda=-(hc/\lambda^2)\Delta \lambda$$
