"Consider a snapshot of a transverse, ideal wave moving along the + x -direction with displacements along the y direction. At a given time t , which section of the string will have the greatest transverse acceleration magnitude ?" I completely understand why the "The section with largest transverse spatial curvature magnitude" is the correct answer but I don't understand why "The section with largest transverse displacement magnitude" is also not a valid answer as well. The two don't seem mutually exclusive.

Reading Quiz 4, question 6: The two are not necessarily exclusive, but they are not necessarily the same, either. Imagine an 'M'-shaped wave form. The tops of the 'M' have the largest displacement, but if these tops are gradually rounded, their associated acceleration would be smaller than a sharp valley in the center of the 'M'. For such a shape, the largest acceleration could even be a location where the displacement is zero!
