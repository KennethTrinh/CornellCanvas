The energy levels of a harmonic potential energy have the form $E_n = (n+ 1/2)\hbar \omega$ for $n$ = 0, 1, 2, etc. So listing out the energy levels, you have $E$ = $(1/2)\hbar \omega$, $(3/2)\hbar \omega$, $(5/2)\hbar \omega$, etc. The spacing between adjacent energy levels is therefore $\hbar \omega$, but the ground state energy is half that value.
