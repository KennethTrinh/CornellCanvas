Author: Glenn Case (admin)

Vote Count: 1

The lower case 'n' refers to the 'order' in the diffraction pattern. First order refers to the maximum closest to the central maximum. Second order refers to the maximum at the next larger angle away from the central maximum, and so on. Resolvance is improved in higher orders for which the angular separation of maxima always increases.
