I think the issue is your value for the amplitude of the standing wave. If the standing wave has a maximum amplitude of 0.002 m, this means the formula for the displacement should be y\left(t\right)=\left(0.002\ m\right)\cos\left(kx+\phi\right)\cos\left(\omega t\right) It looks like you are off by a factor of 2 on the amplitude, and therefore a factor of 4 for the maximum kinetic energy.
