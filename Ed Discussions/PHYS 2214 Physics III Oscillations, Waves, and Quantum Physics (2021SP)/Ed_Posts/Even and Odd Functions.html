Author: Dan Ralph (admin)

Example A has a nonzero average value, so the Fourier series will contain a constant term in addition to sine components. A constant term is an even component (you can think of it as the $a_0$ term in the cosine expansion as in Eq. (9.4) of the Course Notes).
