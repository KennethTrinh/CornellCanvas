Based just on this information, it is hard to diagnose what you are doing. What is the expression for the transmission coefficient $T$ that you are evaluating in part 4e? One thing you should note is that your answer for $T$ should not be the same as what is listed in Table 8-1 in the Course Notes. In that table, the coefficients $R$ and $T$ given describe the relative amplitudes of the reflected and transmitted pressure amplitudes, which will be different than the relative amplitudes of the reflected and transmitted displacement amplitudes that you are considering in this problem.
