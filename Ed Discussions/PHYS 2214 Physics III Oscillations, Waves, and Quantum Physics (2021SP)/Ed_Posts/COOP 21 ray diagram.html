Your upper diagram shows correctly image of the converging lens. This image would only form if the diverging lens were not there--I call it a "provisional" image. One of your rays on the lower image is fine, the other is not. The correct ray is the one that comes from the center of the diverging lens towards the "provisional" image 1. That particular ray is unaffected by the diverging lens, so it comes from the converging lens to image 1 without refraction. The solid ray you drew is wrong because the ray travels from left to right, not from right to left. The horizontal ray from the converging lens should be refracted down to the right upon encountering the diverging lens.
