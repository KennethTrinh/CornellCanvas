You should do the integral. For the velocity, expand $\sin(\omega t+\phi)$ into the form $A\cos(\omega t) +B\sin(\omega t)$ using a trig identity. After multiplying by the force you will get a $\cos^2(\omega t)$ and a $\cos(\omega t)\sin(\omega t)$, both of which are easy to integrate.
