This is a correct approach. If you evaluate the phase velocity $v = \omega/k = (7.5 \times 10^4 (1/s))/(2.5 \times 10^{-4} (1/m)) = 3 \times 10^8$ m/s. This is the speed of light in vacuum, so with $v = c/n$ you would get simply that $n=1$.
