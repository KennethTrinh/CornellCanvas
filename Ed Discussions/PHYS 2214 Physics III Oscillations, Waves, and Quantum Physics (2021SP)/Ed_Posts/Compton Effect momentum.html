It is true that the momentum is related to the wavelength as $p=h/\lambda$, but the wavelength changes during Compton scattering so the final wavelength (and momentum) are different than the initial wavelength (and momentum). The change in the wavelength can be expressed as written in Eq. (20-14) and (20-15) of the Course Notes.
