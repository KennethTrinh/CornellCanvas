The "slow" modulation envelope for beats goes like $s_{max}(x,t) = cos((\Delta k/2)x -(\Delta \omega/2)t)$, which has an effective frequency of $| \Delta f |/2$ or $| f_2 - f_1 |/2$. However, you will have constructive interference of the two waves contributing to the beats and a large amplitude in the beat signal both when $s_{max}(x,t) = 1$ and also -1, so you have intensity maxima twice per cycle of $s_{max}(x,t)$. Because of this, the beat frequency is $| f_2 - f_1 |$, not $| f_2 - f_1 |/2$. Please see Eq. (6-18) in the Course Notes.
