$\epsilon_0$ and $\mu_0$ are fundamental constant properties of vacuum. "Permittivity" $\epsilon_0$ is $8.85\times 10^{-12}$ C^2/(N m^2) . "Permeability" $\mu_0$ is $4\pi\times 10^{-7}$ (T m)/A . The coefficients K are pure numbers (no dimensional units) that differ by material and describe the factor by which the permittivity and permeability differ from their vacuum values in matter.
