You actually don't need to know the force here. If the total energy of the oscillator is equal to $k_B T$, what is the maximum potential energy from a classical point of view as it oscillates back and forth? If you know the maximum potential energy and you know the effective spring constant (labeled by $k$ in this problem), can you calculate $y_{max}$?
