Author: Glenn Case (admin)

No. The 9th harmonic overtone would be a frequency 10x that of the fundamental 16.35 Hz of C_0, or only a frequency of 163.5 Hz. Each subscripted 'C' represents a doubling of frequency from the preceding 'C.' So there are nine factors of 2 (2^9) relating C_9 to C_0. (16.35)x(2^9)=8371
