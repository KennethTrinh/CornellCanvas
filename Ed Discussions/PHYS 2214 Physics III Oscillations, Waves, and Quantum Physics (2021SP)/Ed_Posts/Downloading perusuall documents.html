Sorry, but there is no way to download from Perusall. These course notes are copyright-protected property of the university, so we are not allowed to make them simply downloadable. You may be able to get a digital copy, but I'm not sure and cannot guarantee it.

What is the nature of your internet access issue? It may make several things (homework and quiz submission, viewing lectures, lab participation, etc) very difficult. If you have concerns about how your unreliable internet access may affect you, you should email Dan Ralph or Glenn Case directly.
