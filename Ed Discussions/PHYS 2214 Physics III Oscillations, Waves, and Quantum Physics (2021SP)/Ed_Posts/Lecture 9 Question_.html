What is Q here?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Glenn Case (admin)

Vote Count: 0

Q is the quality factor of the resonance curve. Because Q is large, the system provides dramatic amplification of frequencies very close to the resonant frequency, and hardly any amplification of all other frequencies.
