Visualizing quantum mechanics can be pretty hard, but there's some great videos that can help one understand what it could look like. This video by Veritasium shows off the work from this MIT hydrodynamics lab on visualizing wave-particle duality, and this video by 3Blue1Brown explains the uncertainty principle in more general terms, which helps to understand its application to quantum mechanics.
