Aah. It's array segment c[0..4]. Therefore, c.length, the length of the list, is 5. But read on. Note that our loop invariant puts the two boundaries markers outside the ? section, something like shown below. Here's a reason for this: It makes proving termination --making progress within the repetend-- easier to see. With this invariant, let b.length = 5, so initially k = -1 and t = 5. In this case, we calculate e = (t+k)/2 to get the middle value. That's e = (5 -1)/2 = 2. 
    0         k               t           b.length
   ---------------------------------------
b |    <= v    |      ?      |    > v    |
