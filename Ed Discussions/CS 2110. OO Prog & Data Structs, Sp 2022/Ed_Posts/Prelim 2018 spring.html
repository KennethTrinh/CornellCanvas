The expressions are evaluated before the || is evaluated. So k == -1 evaluates to true/false, then 3 / (k + 1) is evaluated to some integer, and then 3 / (k + 1) != 4 is evaluated to some boolean value. And Then the || is evaluated on two boolean values.
