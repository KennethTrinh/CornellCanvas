    To be very technical, it depends on OP's meaning of "ON". Are the pixels at the very edge of the circle (furthest away from the center) "on"? I.e. are they the border? Or is the border the infinitely thin line just past them? Actually Swing adopts the latter stance: the border is the infinitely thin line (3rd paragraph). So I guess I would say <= is actually the right answer. But as the other staff answer points out, it won't really matter. We'll accept either answer. Probably only gamers with pixel-level accuracy would ever file a bug report about this. ;)
