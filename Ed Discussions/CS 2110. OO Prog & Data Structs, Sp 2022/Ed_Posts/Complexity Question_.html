Think of what happens at each iteration. At the first iteration, in constant time, you find out whether the letter the word starts with is a child of the root. If so, At the second iteration, in constant time, you find out whether the second letter the word starts with a child of a child of the root. And so on. Progressing down to one more level of the takes constant time. How far down the tree do you have to go? Well, (1) No more than the number k of letters in the word your searching for, and (2) No more than the depth of the tree, which is m.
