I believe the most effective way to work with a partner is working on the assignments together. Whether that means setting up a zoom call and sharing screen or meeting in person, it is important both partners understand every part of the assignment. I do not recommend dividing the assignment in half because then each partner may only understand the few methods they worked on. Note that the entirety of each assignment and its concepts/methods are fair game for exams so you will want to understand them all. When I took this class my partner and I would meet up in person and each have our own java project for the assignment. We would then talk through the methods together and code our solutions on our separate versions so we each got the practice of writing out the code. I found this to be extremely helpful in grasping the material but you may find a different approach works best for you! Hope this helps!
