Author: David Gries (admin)

Vote Count: 0

d[w] is the shortest known distance from the start node to node w.

If w is in the settled set, it is the shortest known distance over all paths. If w is in the frontier set, it is over paths all of whose nodes are in the settled set except w.

wgt(f, w) is the length on edge (f, w).
