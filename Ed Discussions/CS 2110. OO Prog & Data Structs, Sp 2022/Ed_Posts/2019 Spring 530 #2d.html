Author: Andrew Chen (staff)

Vote Count: 0

If you put k on the other side of the line, you will have trouble figuring out the middle value e and changing k or h to make progress toward termination. With k to the left and h to the right, proving progress toward termination is much easier.

So, for binary search, put k to the left and h to the right, ALWAYS.
