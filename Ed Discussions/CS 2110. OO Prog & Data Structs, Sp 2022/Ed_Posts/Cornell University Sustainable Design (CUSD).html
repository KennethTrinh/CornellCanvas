Founded in 2009, Cornell University Sustainable Design (CUSD) is a student run, interdisciplinary project team. We bring together students from across all seven colleges to work alongside renowned faculty and knowledgeable professionals in our mission to create a global, sustainable impact. CUSD is currently working on 10+ projects and has over 200 members. Joining CUSD is a great way to meet students from other majors and colleges. For a list of our projects, please visit our website: https://cusd.cornell.edu/ Information sessions are at 1/24 6:00 PM and 1/27 at 8:00 pm on Zoom at this link: https://cornell.zoom.us/j/96072154580?pwd=U1R4L1JlL3BaVCtKKysvN3Zvd1AzZz09 At our info sessions, you will learn more about the teams and be able to chat with the team leads. Applications to join one of our teams are due 2/1 at 11:59 EST! Apply here: https://forms.gle/4tt3GMP7FTznZxEH9 If you have any questions, please email cusd@cornell.edu
