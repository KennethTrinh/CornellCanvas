u is to the left of the line, k is to the left of the line, the unknown region is decreasing in size (think of this as a sliding window) and we keep decreasing u by 1 until there’s no more unknown and the b[h..u] region represents the unknown region, we know this from the invariant, so to satisfy the precondition u must = k
