Read the specification. Based on the specification, the list rev(h.next) is to be reversed. Well, it is. r points at the first node, containing the 3, that node points at the second node, containing the 6, that node points at the third node, containing the 2, and that node is the last one, its next field is null. Until you learn to understand a recursive call in terms of its specification, you will have trouble with recursion.
