Author: David Gries (admin)

Category: Social

Vote Count: 0

The Rising Sophomore Summer Program in Computer Science (CSMore) was created to broaden the participation of underrepresented minorities and first-generation college students in CS by preparing them to successfully complete CS 2800, CS 3110, and CS 3410. Consequently, freshmen (who have an interest in pursuing a major in CS) are encouraged to submit an online application by Friday, 11 March 2022.

CSMore will be held in-person on 18 July 2022 - 12 August 2022 at Cornell University. Each participant will receive a $1,000 stipend; engage with corporations; attend social activities with faculty; and learn about cutting-edge computing research. If you have any questions, contact Dr. LeeAnn Roberts at leeann.roberts@cornell.edu or Professor Eva Tardos at eva.tardos@cornell.edu .
