Are you an underclassman student interested in computer or information science? Want to get to know professors across a broad range of disciplines including game design, machine learning, theory, operations research, information science and more? Hoping to make friends who are also passionate about CIS? Join the WICC Lunch Bunch Program! Geared especially toward underclassmen women and minorities, this program provides a fantastic opportunity to meet with a small group of peers once a week for a conversation with a Cornell professor. Lunch Bunch will run for 9 weeks from February 28th to May 10th. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us, Tanvi Namjoshi and Anna Nguyen, personally at tkn24@cornell.edu and axn3@cornell.edu.

Applications Due February 14th | Apply Here: https://bit.ly/lunch-bunch-sp22
