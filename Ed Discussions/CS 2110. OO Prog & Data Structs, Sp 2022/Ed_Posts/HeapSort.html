Hi, Why is the call to bubbleDown not bubbleDown(b, 0) after you swap values in position 0 and k in b? I thought since b[0..k] is the maxHeap, b[0] is not the right value, so if you bubble it down, it will be a Max heap again.

You want it to be a max-heap! Note the k= k-1; between the swap and the bubbledown. It starts with b[0..k] a heap; Swaps the largest value into b[k], Subtracts 1 from k so that that b[0..k] now refers to a heap (almost) that has one less value; and Bubbles down, making it into a heap.
