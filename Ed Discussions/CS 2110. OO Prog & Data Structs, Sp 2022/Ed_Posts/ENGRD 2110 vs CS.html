It is recommended that you enroll in ENGRD, as mentioned in lecture because the process later down the line will become simpler (if you enroll in CS 2110 instead of ENGRD, you might have to clarify to the college that you are using this course to satisfy that ENGRD requirement for the Computer Science major). However, after speaking with an advisor, as Tomer mentioned, it really does not make a difference and you will satisfy the requirement no matter which one you enroll in. Change it to ENGRD 2110 if it is not too much of a hassle.
