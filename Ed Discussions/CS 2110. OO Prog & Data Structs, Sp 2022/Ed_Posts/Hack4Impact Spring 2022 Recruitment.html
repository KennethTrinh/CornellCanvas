Hack4Impact Cornell is a team of undergraduate students who care about improving the world around us with technology by building technology for social good. Each semester, we partner with nonprofits and social-good organizations to build projects that solve problems in the sectors of criminal justice, transportation, sustainability, and more. All of our members are enrolled in 2 graded credits. To learn more about what we do and why you should join, visit our website cornellh4i.netlify.app and Instagram @cornellhack4impact.
