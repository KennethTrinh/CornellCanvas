Hack4Impact Cornell is a team of undergraduate students who care about improving the world around us with technology by building technology for social good. Each semester, we partner with nonprofits and social-good organizations to build projects that solve problems in the sectors of criminal justice, transportation, sustainability, and more. All of our members are enrolled in 2 graded credits. To learn more about what we do and why you should join, visit our website cornellh4i.netlify.app and Instagram @cornellhack4impact. Find more detailed descriptions of the roles (development, product design, business) here: bit.ly/h4i-descriptions. Find more information about the organizations and projects for Spring 2022 here: https://bit.ly/h4i-sp22-projects Info Sessions (all virtual): Wednesday, January 26th, 6-7 PM EST Monday, January 31st, 5:30-6:30 PM EST RSVP here: https://bit.ly/h4i-sp22-info Apply here: https://bit.ly/h4i-sp22-app Sign up for coffee chats here (optional): https://bit.ly/h4i-chats Applications close on Thursday, February 3rd at 11:59 PM EST. Within 2-3 days of the deadline, our executive board will reach out to applicants with next steps.
