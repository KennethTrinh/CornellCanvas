Why is the answer to 3(b) i=k? Shouldn't it be i=k+1 so that there is no element in the sorted section?

It is correct. Think of these facts. 1. The precondition says that h <= k. Therefore the segment b[h..k] has at least one element. 2. Making i = k initially means that the segment b[h..k] has ONE element, and a one-element segment is sorted, so the invariant is true. Now, one CAN start with i = k+1. That just means the invariant is true with segment b[i..k] empty.
