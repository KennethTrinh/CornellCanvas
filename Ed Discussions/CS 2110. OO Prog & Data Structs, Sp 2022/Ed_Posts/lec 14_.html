This statement has a conditional expression. Let me parenthesize it:

int uc= (h+1==n || b[h] > b[h+1) ? h : h+1);

Now, we know that h < n. That means that the left child of k exists. Three cases arise:

1. h+1 = n. Then, k has only ONE left child in the heap. So n is bigger one and h is stored in uk.

2. h+1 < n. Aah. k has two children in the heap. The bigger one must be stored in uc.

2A. b[h] > b[h+1]. So b[h] is bigger, and h is stored in uc.

2B. b[h] <= b[h+1]. So b[h+1] is bigger, and h+1 is stored in uc.
