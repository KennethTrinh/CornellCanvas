Below is a graph with 4 nodes ---A, B, C, D-- and two edges: (A,B) and (C,D).

It consists of two connected components, but the graph itself is not connected. e.g. there is no way to get from node A to node C.

The graph is not a tree. It consists of TWO trees. We could call it a forest.
