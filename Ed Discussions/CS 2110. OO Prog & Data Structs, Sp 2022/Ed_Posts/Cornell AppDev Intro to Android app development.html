Cornell AppDev, an open-source app development project team, is teaching an S/U course for prospective students to learn how to develop apps for the Android ecosystem using Kotlin and understand how apps function in relation to the OS and the internet. After taking the course you will have some industry-level hands-on development experience and you will have mastered a new coding language Kotlin. The course starts on February 21, takes 1-2 credits. If interested, take a survey with the following link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe9G15l6Ju88pccNAhXIGmqGxkq_mOuedeB0Odka0gkjClfeg/viewform?usp=sf_link

and enroll in the CS1998-602 course.
