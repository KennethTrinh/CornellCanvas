Below is part of the specification of equals:

"for every t1 in s1..." tells you to write a foreach loop over elements of s1.

You have to figure out what the body of the loop should do, returning if something isn't true. Use of the suggested helper function will help.

(3) their children sets are equal.<br> * ------ Let the two children sets be s1 and s2. * ------ Since their sizes are equal, this requires: * ---------- for every CovidTree t1 in s1 there is a CovidTree t2 in s2 for which t1.equals(t2) is true.<br>
