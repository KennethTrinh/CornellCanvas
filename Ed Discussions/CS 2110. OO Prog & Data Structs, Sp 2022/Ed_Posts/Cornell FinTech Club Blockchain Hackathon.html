Cornell FinTech Club (CFT) is hosting a blockchain hackathon at Warren Hall from April 29 to May 1, 2022. The event will be sponsored by Ava Labs, Fintech at Cornell, and The Initiative for CryptoCurrencies and Contracts (IC3).

This hackathon aims to inspire participants to create blockchain innovations by introducing fintech concepts and developing on Avalanche, the smart contracts blockchain platform maintained by Ava Labs. Attendees can mentor, judge, or host/speak at a workshop/event.

With workshops, a $6k+ prize pool, multiple challenges, food, swag, and social and corporate events, attendees and Cornell undergraduate hackers in teams of 3-5 will experience a fun, innovative weekend on campus.

No coding experience is needed, any skillset is welcome at a hackathon and workshops will teach coding, design, and business skills too. Come and learn how to make innovations in blockchain! Spots are limited, register today! https://tinyurl.com/CFThackathon

Any questions please don't hesitate to reach out to cornellfintechclub@gmail.com , thank you!
