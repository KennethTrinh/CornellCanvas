mGen = 1 + Math.max ( mGen , s.MaxGen());

The line you proposed sets mGen equal to 1 plus at least mGen (i.e., mGen, or else the other, larger, quantity). Equivalently, for each iteration in the loop, the line sets mGen equal to mGen plus at least 1. So, by the end of the loop, you have incremented mGen for every immediate child, which is not what you intended to do. So, no, this line does not work.
