https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&rls=en&q=Eclipse+%22This+compilation+unit+is+not+on+the+build+path+of+a+Java+project.%22&ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8

It may be that your did not create the Java project correctly. One fix I found by googling is the following. Try it and let us know whether it worked:

  • Right-Click on your project.

  • Go to "Properties"

  • Choose "Project Natures"

  • Click on "Add"

  • Choose "Java"

  • Click "Apply and Close"
