Below are some YouTube videos that we found appropriate during these trying times. Some are just happy and help bring us together, others show how people are coping with social separation, and others show us with song and dance what we should be doing to help stop Covid-19. Feel free to add you own links to music as a follow-up. This has been a favorite of ours for years: Stand By Me : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Us-TVg40ExM What a wonderful world this could be : In the second, Louis Armstrong's message from the 1970's still resonates: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ddLd0QRf7Vg https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2nGKqH26xlg André Rieu & his orchestra performing You'll Never Walk Alone in Maastricht. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x3aGlKYlEiY This is a song about every-day people, living together: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-g4UWvcZn5U Here's a gospel song, "The Storm is Passing Over". You don't have to be a Christian to enjoy this rendition and its message, to experience some of the joy the performers and audience feel. The conductor is like no other. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d3jgPsGQSdQ Songs about Covid-19 Look at how the Italians responded from their apartment balconies to their quarantine. One person said, "The reason I think we do it is that we feel like we're all one, and not separated like before." https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EBByYjjvNzs Italians sing "Bella Ciao" from their windows and balconies: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccibC8o2_l8 And then the Germans follow up to support the Italians: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z5CrScIHAuE This Corona Song tells people to be careful and wash their hands. It has been translated into many different languages. Use the subtitles /closed captions feature. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BtulL3oArQw Here's a catchy danced about protecting yourself against the corona virus. It's done by the Vietnamese: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bqx19f0ceUo Here's a song by the Jain's titled “Corona Se Darona” about Jainism and the corona virus.
