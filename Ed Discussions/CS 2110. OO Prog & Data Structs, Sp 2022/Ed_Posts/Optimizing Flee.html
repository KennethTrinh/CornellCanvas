You have access to all of the nodes on the map for flee with one of the methods provided. Given a node n, if n.getTile().gold()!=0 then the tile corresponding to the node has gold. You collect the gold by going on top of the tile. Storing things in variables is a personal matter and you can decide how you want to store data. There are many ways one can go about this.
