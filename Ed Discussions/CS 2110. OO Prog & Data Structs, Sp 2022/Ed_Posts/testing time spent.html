Author: Andrew Chen (staff)

Vote Count: 1

At most n times means the worst-case scenario is n iterations, so the time complexity is linear. Constant time would be a constant number of operations independent of n. For more info, I would look back at the corresponding lectures and search up "time complexity" on JHT.
