It has nothing to do with "unknown". You want the loop to stop when b[h..j] is empty. That section has this many elements in it: j+1-h. Therefore, when j+1-h = 0, the section is empty. That is, when h = j+1. Does that make sense to you? If h = j+1, then b[0..h-1] <= 0. and b[h..k] >= 0.
