The whole point of quicksort is so that the numbers to the left of the pivot is less than the pivot and the numbers to the right or the pivot is greater than the pivot, so if your array is already sorted and you choose your pivot point to be the first number (this should be the smallest since it’s already sorted) to the left of the pivot you’ll have no numbers that are less than the pivot since it’s already the smallest
