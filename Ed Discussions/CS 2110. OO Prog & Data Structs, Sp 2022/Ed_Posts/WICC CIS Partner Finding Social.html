The SOCIAL FOR FINDING PARTNERS IS WEDNESDAY, 2 FEBRUARY, TOMORROW, AT 7PM (The Zoom will start at 6:30)!

Please read the following notes carefully so that you can maximize your experience at the event. This is a social, so please be prepared to turn your camera on for the entirety of the event. We highly highly recommend that you use your laptop/desktop for when we switch to Glimpse as we have experienced the best results with this kind of device.

  • 1. Please log onto the zoom (invite pasted below) at 7PM to go over introductions and other announcements.

  • 2. Once directed, the host will copy and paste a bit.ly link in the chat. Please go to that link, which will take you to a website called “Glimpse” This is the primary location for the event.

  • 3. Sign up as a guest —> this step is very important as it will save you time, instead of having to go through the whole sign up process

  • 4. Please fill in any identifying information (name, year, major) and click your respective tags/the tags of whom you want to be matched with!

  • 5. Start matching :)

    • If you run into any questions during matching, please email sh822@cornell.edu, and she will get back to you asap with a solution and/or other instructions. Once matched, you will have 5 minutes per partner . There is an option for ice breakers, games, and other activities if you run out of things to do during this time limit. Please do not switch partners in the middle of these 5 minutes as it will mess up the experience for other participants. If comfortable, you may use the “share socials” portion of glimpse to exchange any social media information.
