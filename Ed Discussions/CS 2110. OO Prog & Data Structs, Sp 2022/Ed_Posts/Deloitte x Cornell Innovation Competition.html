Deloitte Innovation Competition is a Hackathon-style event in which students form, or join, a team to propose a technology related improvement, solution, or opportunity for Cornell. Once selected, student teams will work one-on-one with Deloitte practitioners over a one-week period (April 20th – April 27th) to prepare for the Final Pitch on April 29th. The judges are comprised of Cornell faculty and Deloitte Consulting leadership. This event is open to all undergraduates and non-MBA Master’s students. Students can sign-up via this link: Cornell Innovation Competition Survey 2022 (deloitte.com) https://deloittesurvey.deloitte.com/Community/se/3FC11B262274B958
