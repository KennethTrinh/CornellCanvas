Hello! I am confused about what it means to cast a variable to an interface. Does it lose its implementation?

----------- REPLIES -----------

Author: Tomer Shamir (staff)

Vote Count: 0

Casting is just a way to change the way the compiler views some variable. It doesn't change the implementation at all. Based on the bottom-up rule, the implementation used when calling methods will still be the same
