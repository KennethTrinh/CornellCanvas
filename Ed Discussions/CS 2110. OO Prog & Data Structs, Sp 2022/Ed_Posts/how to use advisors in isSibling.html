To test whether two PhDs are siblings because their first advisors are the same,

1. Create a PhD named AD that will be the advisor.

2. Create a PHD p1 and make AD its first advisor.

3. Create a PHD p2 and make AD its first advisor.

Now do an assertEquals with first argument true and second argument p1.isSiblings(p2).
