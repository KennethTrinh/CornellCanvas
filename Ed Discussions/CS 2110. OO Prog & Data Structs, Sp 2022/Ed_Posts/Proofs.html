Because of the definition of big o notation, we want to get to the form c*g(n), where g(n) is 10+n^4. Since we already have 10n^4, adding 100 gets us to 10n^4 + 100 — which is 10*g(n), proving that f(n) is O(g(n)) thanks to the definition.
