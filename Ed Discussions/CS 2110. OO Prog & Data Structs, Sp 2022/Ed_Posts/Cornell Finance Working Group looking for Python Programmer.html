Are you interested in Programming, Finance, and Nature? This opportunity might be for you!

Please reach out if you are comfortable with Python (most likely will need to use the Python package "Beautiful Soup" or "Selenium") and are interested in being compensated for your work.

This is a great way to become involved with research on campus and put your programming skills to good use!
