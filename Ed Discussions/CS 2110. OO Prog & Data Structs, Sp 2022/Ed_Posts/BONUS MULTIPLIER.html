The bonus depends on how "long" (steps) the find phase takes. From page 2 of the handout:

There is no time limit for this task, but you will receive a higher score bonus multiplier for finding the Orb in fewer steps.

Next time you run the program, look at the data panel. It shows Gold, Bonus (multiplier), and Score. The score is the product of Gold and Bonus.

Sorry this isn't clear.
