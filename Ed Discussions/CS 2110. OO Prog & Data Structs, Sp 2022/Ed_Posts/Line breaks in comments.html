For Javadoc comments, you should use <br>. For other multiline comments, you can't use that tag to break the line, so it's ok if those go a bit over the 80 character limit.

Author: David Gries (admin)

You should learn to use <br> to make your javadoc comments as readable as possible. For example, it is always best to have Preconditions on a separate line, so put <br> just before it.

Don't ask "am I absolutely required to ...". Instead, ask "What are best practices? I want to learn as much as possible about good professional programming." You are here to learn, not simply to get a grade.
