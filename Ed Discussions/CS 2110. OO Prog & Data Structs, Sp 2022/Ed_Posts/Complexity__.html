Author: David Gries (admin)

Compute n = 2^k by increased doubling multiplying by 2. Look at the table below. To calculate 32 = 2^5 by doubling took 5 steps. log (32) = log(2^5) = 5. 

value     number of times doubled
 1 = 2^0         0
 2 = 2^1         1
 4 = 2^2         2
 8 = 2^3         3
16 = 2^4         4
32 = 2^5         5
